🎯 What is this site

Damage (DPS) and build calculator forThe Division 2. What it can do:
  • Calculate DPS using real numbers from the game menu
  • Store your builds locally + publish to a shared directory
  • Watch/like/fork other people's builds from the Community
  • Compare, calculate TTK for 5 difficulties and Escalation Tier
  • Advise what to download next (stat weights)

🚀 How to build in 5 steps

  1. Open a tab⚙ BUILD
  2. Click— pick a weapon —and find your gun (exotic/nominal/regular)
  3. Fill 6 slots with items: mask, bib, backpack, gloves, holster, knee pads
  4. If the chestpiece/backpack is normal, select their talents in the selectors
  5. In the block"Statistics from the game"enter the total values ​​from the weapon menu in the game (the “Offensive” tab): CHC, CHD, HSD, WD, etc.
💡 The DPS result will appear at the top of the block⚡ Build total(Base / Average / Peak).

💡 What are buckets (multipliers)

Damage is calculated as a product of independent multipliers. Each is a separate “bucket”.Insidebucket values ​​add up,betweenbuckets - multiply.
Damage = Base × WD × Crit × HS × DtA × OoC × Amp × Expertise
Bucket What's inside Example
WDTotal weapon damage + damage by type (LMG/AR/SMG...)90% + 30% → ×2.20
CreteCHC × CHD (average multiplier)55% × 150% → ×1.83
HSDHead Damage × Hit %145% × 30% → ×1.43
DtADamage to armor (max ~20%)+15% → ×1.15
OoCOutside cover (max ~30%)+10% → ×1.10
AmpAmplified (Stryker 4pc, some exotics)+20% → ×1.20
ExpertiseSeparate multiplier on top of WD+15 lvl → ×1.15

❓ FAQ

Why is my DPS not equal to the number in the game menu?
The game menu only showsBase × WDno crits, no headshots, no stacks. The calculator countsfulldamage on full stacks, taking into account all buckets - hence the “Peak DPS” is much higher.
What is SHD Watch / Skiir's Watch?
With SHD 1000+ you get perm bonuses: +10% WD, +20% HSD, +10% CHC, +20% CHD + control. Enter them in the ⌚ SHD Watch block - they will be added to the total. Total CHC cap - 60%.
What counts from the Y8S1 Recombinator?
Weapons only go into DPSHeadshot damage and Rounds(per magazine). Defensive and utility modifiers shown for reference — not counted in DPS.
What is "Base/Average/Peak DPS"?
Base— DPS without accumulated stacks (first bullet in battle).
Average— DPS for 10 seconds of battle (stacks are built up gradually).
Peak— DPS on full stacks (final phase of the battle).
What do the tags on the build card mean?
Tags show composition:Striker ×4— 4 set items,Petrov— 1 brand item,✦ Salvo- personal,🧿 Mantis- exotic.

⚠️ What calculator does NOT compute

We either simplify these game mechanics or don’t model them at all. If your build is based on them, the real DPS will be different:
  • Specialization signature weapons(TAC-50, Crossbow, Sticky Bomb, Eagle Sentry, etc.) — separate weapons with unique math, not counted.
  • Linked Laser Pointer + Pulse(Technician spec) — pulsed enemies take +20% damage from Kingbreaker AR; this combo is not in the calculator.
  • Headhunter (Serial Killer)— the talent transfers base × N shots to the next hits. Complex "passthrough" mechanics - simplified as a regular talent.
  • Overdogs +30% for weak class— we cannot distinguish between enemies of different classes (hunter, renegade, grenade launcher); count as amp.
  • NPC faction resistance- Hunters/Black Tusk have different armor, Pyromaniac bonuses on Cleaners are stronger. There is select, but there is no logic.
  • Specific group buffs— Beacon shares the bonus with allies, Bittersweet heals. Only the player himself is taken into account.
  • Burst talents (Merciless 800%/primer)are shown as a separate bar as “BURST single shot”, but are not integrated into sustained DPS.
  • Rolling crit / chain crit— we do not model complex patterns (5 in a row → next bonus).
  • Skill-builds: linear tier scaling— we use a linear multiplier ×1.18/dash. The actual formula may not be linear for some skills.
  • Recombinator incomplete— we only take into account head damage and ammo. Other modifiers (protective/utility) are for reference.

💡 If any of this is important for your build - write🐛 Bug report, we will add the mechanic.
