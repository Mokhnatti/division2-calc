## Tip of the Spear — Specialty Weapons and Grenade Spam Guide

Tip of the Spear is the gear set built around specialization weapons and grenades — the often-overlooked "third pillar" of damage in The Division 2. While most builds focus on weapons or skills, Tip of the Spear cranks up the damage on signature weapons and explosive utility, turning the underused Crossbow, Minigun, and TAC-50 into legitimate primary damage sources.

In TU22, Tip of the Spear remains the go-to set for grenade-focused builds and signature-weapon spam, and it has surprisingly strong group utility in Heroic and Legendary content.

### At a Glance

Tip of the Spear is a red brand set with a unique identity: it amplifies your specialization weapon and grenade damage, then refreshes specialty ammo on kill. The 4-piece transforms specializations from situational tools into central kill engines.

- Brand color: Red (grenade / specialization)
- Slots: 6 (Mask, Backpack, Chest, Gloves, Holster, Kneepads)
- Chest talent: Dragon's Mouth
- Backpack talent: Free slot — Vigilance or Bloodlust recommended
- Best content: Heroic open world, Manhunt, Iron Horse, Countdown
- TU22 patch status: Stable, slight refresh-rate buff

### Set Bonuses Detail

- 2-piece: +25% Specialization Damage. This applies to your signature weapon (Crossbow, Minigun, TAC-50, Sticky Bomb sig from Demolitionist, etc.).
- 3-piece: +30% Grenade Damage. Applies to all grenades — frag, incendiary, EMP, shock, fire-starter mods.

The 2-piece is the more meaningful bonus for most builds since signature weapons are limited-ammo, high-impact tools. The 3-piece grenade bonus stacks with grenade-damage rolls on gear, allowing Tip of the Spear builds to throw 1M+ damage frags against elite groups.

### 4pc Mechanic — Dragon's Mouth

Dragon's Mouth is the chest talent that makes Tip of the Spear viable as a primary build rather than a utility splash.

- Every kill with your specialization weapon refreshes specialty ammo
- Specifically: 1 ammo refunded per signature kill
- Works with all signature weapon types

This means a Sharpshooter with the TAC-50 can chain headshot kills indefinitely, a Demolitionist can keep the Minigun roaring through entire elite waves, and a Survivalist Crossbow build can keep the explosive bolts flowing. The refresh applies to elite kills, not just adds, so the build scales into Heroic and Legendary where each elite kill matters.

The mechanic combines with the 2-piece +25% damage to create a feedback loop: stronger sig damage means more kills, more kills means more sig ammo, which means more damage. Against waves of equal-tier enemies, the build essentially has unlimited signature uptime.

### Best Weapons

Tip of the Spear builds use weapons primarily as backup. The signature weapon is the star, but you still need a primary for non-sig kills.

- Eagle Bearer (exotic AR): The flexible primary choice. High DPS for non-sig moments.
- Bullet King (exotic LMG): Pairs well for Demolitionist Minigun builds since both share the high-volume identity.
- Famas 2010 (named AR): Strong primary that doesn't compete with the sig for stat priority.
- Lefty (named pistol): Niche but works for Sharpshooter swap-fire setups.
- Sweet Dreams (named shotgun): Close-range primary for Crossbow Survivalist builds.

For grenades, the specific grenade type matters less than the damage roll. Frag grenades scale highest with the 3-piece bonus, while incendiary grenades benefit from status synergy if you splash with Ongoing Directive.

### Top Builds

#### Tip of Spear × Demolitionist (Minigun Spam)

The most aggressive Tip of the Spear build. Demolitionist's Minigun signature, with refresh-on-kill from the 4-piece, becomes a near-permanent damage tool. The +25% sig damage from 2-piece pushes the Minigun's already-strong damage into broken territory in Heroic content.

Loadout: Tip of Spear 4-piece + Eagle Bearer + Vigilance backpack
Specialization: Demolitionist
Backpack: Vigilance
Skills: Striker Drone + Reviver Hive
Stat priority: Weapon Damage, then Explosive Damage on backpack

In Heroic open world, you can hold the Minigun trigger through entire waves, refreshing ammo as you melt elites. Group play loves this build for crowd control.

#### Tip of Spear × Sharpshooter (TAC-50 Burst)

The marksman variant. The TAC-50 signature with +25% damage and refresh-on-kill becomes a one-tap tool for Black Tusk snipers and named elites in Manhunt.

Loadout: Tip of Spear 4-piece + Mantis + Glass Cannon backpack
Specialization: Sharpshooter
Backpack: Glass Cannon
Skills: Sniper Turret + Reviver Hive
Stat priority: Headshot Damage, Weapon Damage, Explosive Damage

This build excels at high-value-target removal in Heroic and Legendary content, where the TAC-50 can one-shot priority threats and refresh ammo immediately.

### Pros and Cons

Pros

- Only set that meaningfully buffs specialization damage
- Refresh-on-kill creates effectively unlimited sig uptime
- Strong group utility through grenade pressure
- Pairs well with multiple specializations (Demo, Sharpshooter, Survivalist)
- Excellent in Heroic open world for elite waves
- Less reliant on weapon talent rolls

Cons

- Weak against single-target boss fights with no add waves (no refresh source)
- Specialization-locked: weak choice for skill builds or pure weapon builds
- Lower personal weapon DPS than dedicated red sets
- Requires positioning awareness for sig deployment
- Grenade bonus is wasted in encounters where grenades are unsafe

### FAQ

Q: Does the refresh-on-kill work on adds or only elites?
A: Both. Any kill with your sig weapon refunds 1 ammo, including basic adds. This makes Heroic Manhunt extremely sustainable.

Q: Can I refresh sig ammo with the TAC-50 against bosses?
A: Only if the boss dies. Phased bosses won't trigger the refresh during damage windows. This is why TAC-50 builds prefer Manhunt over raid encounters.

Q: Does the 3-piece grenade bonus affect mod-applied status effects?
A: It affects the explosion and direct damage of grenades. Status effects scale separately, but incendiary grenades benefit from the burn-damage portion.

Q: How does Tip of the Spear compare to Hard Wired or other skill sets?
A: Tip of the Spear is sig-focused, not skill-focused. The two sets don't really compete; Tip of Spear is its own niche.

Q: Best specialization for the set?
A: Demolitionist for sustained DPS, Sharpshooter for burst HVT removal, Survivalist for crossbow pressure. Demolitionist is most popular due to the Minigun's add-clear power.

Q: Can I run Tip of the Spear in Conflict (PvP)?
A: It's situational. The grenade bonus is nice, but PvP rarely lets you set up clean sig shots.

Q: What's the gear stat priority?
A: Weapon Damage on cores, then Explosive Damage on attributes. The set's identity already provides the sig and grenade scaling.

### Closing

Tip of the Spear is the most underutilized red set in The Division 2, and that's a shame because it offers a genuinely unique playstyle that rewards aggressive sig deployment and grenade pressure. The Dragon's Mouth refresh-on-kill mechanic transforms specializations from cooldown-bound utility into sustained damage tools, and the grenade bonus creates strong group support in Heroic content. If you've been ignoring your signature weapon, Tip of the Spear gives you a reason to bring it back to the front of your build identity.
