## True Patriot — Group Debuff Support DPS Guide

True Patriot is the most cooperative gear set in The Division 2. Built around the iconic red, white, and blue debuff cycle, it transforms a single agent into a force multiplier for the entire team. While solo players often skip True Patriot in favor of pure DPS sets, in group play — especially raids and Heroic/Legendary directives — it elevates everyone else's damage in a way no other set can match.

This guide explains exactly how the debuff stack works, when True Patriot is mandatory, and how to build it for both raid support and solo viability.

### At a Glance

True Patriot is a red brand set, but its identity is not direct DPS — it's debuff application. The 4-piece talent applies a rotating set of effects that punish enemies for being hit, then explode into a damage-amp window for your allies.

- Brand color: Red (debuff / support)
- Slots: 6 (Mask, Backpack, Chest, Gloves, Holster, Kneepads)
- Chest talent: Full Flag (debuff cycle)
- Backpack talent: Free slot — Vigilance or Perfectly Glass Cannon
- Best content: Operation Dark Hours, Iron Horse, group Manhunt, Conflict ranked
- TU22 patch status: Stable, slight buff to debuff duration

### Set Bonuses Detail

- 2-piece: +15% damage to targets out of cover. This is a meaningful weapon damage bonus against any enemy not snapped to a barricade. It applies to all your shots, not just full-flag-active targets.
- 3-piece: +20% reload speed. This is a quality-of-life bonus that becomes critical on the 4-piece because rapid magazine cycling drives debuff stacking.

The 2-piece bonus rewards aggressive positioning that flushes enemies out of cover, while the 3-piece keeps your debuff uptime high.

### 4pc Mechanic — Full Flag

Full Flag is the heart of True Patriot. As you land hits on a target, you apply colored debuffs in sequence: Red, White, Blue. Each one triggers when the previous color fills up. Once all three colors are active simultaneously, the target enters the "Full Flag" state, which is when allies gain massive damage amps.

Effects:

- Red debuff (first applied): The target takes +20% damage from all sources.
- White debuff (second applied): All allies near the target gain +20% damage.
- Blue debuff (third applied): The target's outgoing damage is reduced by 50%.

When all three are active, the cumulative effect is +20% damage to target, +20% group damage, and -50% target damage — effectively a 60% offensive swing in one debuff cycle.

The debuffs decay over time, but reapplying any color refreshes the cycle. Sustained-fire weapons keep the flag flying constantly.

### Best Weapons

True Patriot wants weapons that fire consistently to drive debuff application. Single-shot rifles and snipers are weak picks; high-magazine, high-fire-rate weapons dominate.

- Bullet King (exotic LMG): The premier True Patriot pairing. Infinite magazine means non-stop debuff cycling, with Full Flag uptime approaching 100%.
- Stop Sign (named LMG): Excellent alternative with high RPM.
- Eagle Bearer (exotic AR): Great fit for raid encounters where you alternate between debuff application and personal DPS.
- ACS-12 (full-auto shotgun): Strong close-range Full Flag generator.
- Famas 2010 (named AR): High RoF makes it a debuff machine.

Avoid: Marksman rifles (too few hits per second), shotguns with low pellet counts, and any weapon with magazines under 30.

### Top Builds

#### True Patriot × Bullet King (Sustained Debuff)

This is the canonical True Patriot raid build. Bullet King's no-reload mechanic means you can hold Full Flag indefinitely on a primary target. Pair with offensive backpack and gloves for personal DPS during the cycle.

Loadout: True Patriot 4-piece + Bullet King + offensive backpack
Backpack: Vigilance (you'll be at full armor often as a back-line debuffer)
Skills: Striker Drone + Striker Turret (additional pressure on debuffed target)
Stat priority: Weapon Damage, CHD, then Critical Hit Chance to cap

In raid encounters, you'll hold the flag on the boss and watch your team's numbers double.

#### True Patriot × Eagle Bearer

This is the more flexible option for non-raid content. The Eagle Bearer's exotic talent gains stacks on hits, and True Patriot's debuff cycle plus reload speed feeds those stacks faster than any other AR pairing.

Loadout: True Patriot 4-piece + Eagle Bearer + perfectly Glass Cannon backpack
Backpack: Glass Cannon
Skills: Reviver Hive + Striker Drone
Stat priority: Weapon Damage stacks, then CHD

This build is the go-to for Heroic Manhunt and Summit floor 80+, where you need both group support and personal kill power.

### Pros and Cons

Pros

- Best support set in the game for raid encounters
- 60% effective damage swing on Full Flag uptime
- Reload speed bonus is universally useful
- The Blue debuff (-50% target damage) is a defensive godsend in Legendary content
- Pairs with virtually any high-magazine weapon
- Two-piece bonus rewards aggressive positioning

Cons

- Solo-weak compared to pure DPS sets — no allies means White debuff is wasted
- Debuff cycle takes time to set up; not great for instant burst
- Boss enemies with damage-immune phases can break the rotation
- Magazine-dependent — bad fit for sniper or shotgun builds
- Lower personal DPS ceiling than Striker or Umbra
- Requires teammates who know to capitalize on White debuff windows

### FAQ

Q: Does Full Flag affect named bosses with damage caps?
A: Yes, the +20% Red debuff applies before damage caps. The Blue debuff also reduces incoming damage from those bosses, which is huge in Legendary directives.

Q: Can two players run True Patriot together?
A: Yes, and they stack independently. Each player applies their own debuff cycle, and the White debuff buffs cross-amp with each other. This is occasionally used in coordinated raid setups.

Q: Does the 2-piece "out of cover" bonus apply to bosses?
A: Yes, named bosses are typically considered out of cover unless they have specific cover-snap animations. Most raid bosses give you the bonus permanently.

Q: How long does Full Flag last?
A: Each debuff color lasts 8 seconds and refreshes on hit. With sustained fire, Full Flag uptime is effectively 100%.

Q: Is True Patriot viable in Conflict (PvP)?
A: Surprisingly yes. The Blue debuff -50% outgoing damage is brutal in 1v1 trades, and ranked teams sometimes run a dedicated Patriot debuffer.

Q: What's the priority order for gear cores?
A: Weapon damage on offensive slots, armor/health on defensive. Reload speed and rate of fire are nice-to-haves on attributes.

Q: Does True Patriot work with skill builds?
A: Not really. The set is built around weapon-driven debuff application. Skill damage doesn't trigger Full Flag.

### Closing

True Patriot is the best support set in The Division 2 and one of the most rewarding to play in coordinated groups. The Full Flag cycle creates a rhythm of pressure that allies can capitalize on, and the Blue debuff alone makes Legendary content survivable for the whole squad. Bring a Bullet King, find groupmates who understand the White debuff window, and you become the agent every raid team wants. Even outside raids, True Patriot remains the go-to choice for team-oriented Manhunt and Heroic farms.
