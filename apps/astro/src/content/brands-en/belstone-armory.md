## Belstone Armory - Pure Tank Brand

Last reviewed: 2026-04-25 · TU22.1 · Verified vs in-game

Belstone Armory is the most defensive brand in The Division 2. Every bonus on every slot pushes armor higher and keeps you alive longer. If you want to run a true tank build, a Bulwark shield pusher, or a full-armor frontline character that can survive Legendary boss melee swings, Belstone is the cornerstone brand.

### At a Glance

- 1pc: +1% Armor Regeneration
- 2pc: +10% Armor on Kill
- 3pc: +20% Total Armor
- Role: Pure Tank
- Tier: S for tank, A for hybrid armor builds
- Best slot count: 3pc
- Synergies: Foundry Bulwark, Walker Harris & Co., Yaahl, Murakami, Heartbreaker

The brand commits hard to one identity: armor recovery. The 1pc gives you steady regen, the 2pc rewards aggressive play with armor chunks per kill, and the 3pc multiplies your total pool. Stacked together, they create the highest passive survivability of any brand combination.

### Bonuses Detail

The 1pc +1% Armor Regeneration is small on paper but massive in practice. Armor regen ticks every second, so 1% of a 1.5 million armor pool is 15,000 per second from this bonus alone. Combined with mod rolls (each armor regen mod adds another flat amount) and the Walker Harris & Co. set, you can get to a sustainable regen tier where you tank shots faster than enemies can chip you down.

The 2pc +10% Armor on Kill is the offense-rewarding layer. Every enemy you kill returns 10% of your max armor. In a Heroic Control Point with 30+ enemies, that means near-permanent full armor as long as you're killing. Pair this with weapons like the M1A Tactical Marksman Rifle, Carbine 7, or any high-magazine LMG to keep the kill stream going.

The 3pc +20% Total Armor is the largest passive armor multiplier in the game from any single brand bonus. It stacks multiplicatively with armor rolls, mods, and chest plate stats. A baseline 1.2 million armor character running 3pc Belstone walks into combat with 1.44 million effective armor before mods.

### Best Use Cases

Belstone shines in four archetypes:

1. Foundry Bulwark shield tank. Foundry's full set bonus increases shield health by armor on kill, and Belstone fuels that loop directly. The 3pc total armor and 2pc on-kill armor make Bulwark shield nearly unbreakable.
2. Heartbreaker burn tank. Heartbreaker scales weapon damage off armor on kill stacks, and Belstone is the cleanest source of that buff plus raw armor.
3. Walker Harris & Co. armor regen. Combine 3pc Belstone with 3pc Walker for two armor regen layers, then add Reassigned mod rolls for sustained tanking.
4. Hybrid tank+DPS. 2pc Belstone slotted into a Petrov LMG or Fenris AR build adds the on-kill armor without sacrificing damage output.

It is not the right pick for skill builds, status builds, or marksman crit chains. Those want Hana-U, Empress, or Airaldi.

### Items in Brand

Belstone drops the standard six gear slots: mask, backpack, chest, gloves, holster, kneepads. Notable rolls per slot:

- Mask: Headshot Damage, Critical Hit Chance, Armor
- Backpack: Weapon Damage, Critical Hit Damage, Armor
- Chest: Armor, Weapon Damage, Health
- Gloves: Weapon Damage, Critical Hit Chance, Armor
- Holster: Critical Hit Damage, Armor, Weapon Damage
- Kneepads: Armor, Hazard Protection, Weapon Damage

The brand has more Armor rolls available across slots than any other brand, which makes it ideal when you're farming for raw EHP.

### Pros & Cons

Pros:
- Highest passive armor scaling in the game
- All three bonuses are useful in any tank archetype
- Synergizes with Foundry, Heartbreaker, Walker, Yaahl
- Roll table favors armor and weapon damage, both useful

Cons:
- Zero skill scaling - useless in pure skill builds
- 1pc Armor Regen is small without mod and brand stacking
- Total Armor doesn't help if you're being one-shot by sniper crits
- Crowded design space - competes with Walker Harris for tank slot

### FAQ

Q: Does 2pc Armor on Kill stack with mod rolls?
A: Yes. The brand bonus and mod rolls both add to the on-kill armor pool, which means you can stack to recover full armor on a single kill in some builds.

Q: Is 1pc Armor Regen worth a slot in a non-tank build?
A: Marginally. The percentage is small without supporting gear. If you're not committing to a tank build, use that slot for Petrov, Fenris or Sokolov instead.

Q: Belstone vs Walker Harris for the tank slot?
A: Walker has +10% Damage to Elites and Bonus Armor, which favors damage output. Belstone has higher raw armor multipliers and on-kill recovery, which favors survival. Most top tank builds run both, splitting 3+3.

Q: Does Belstone work with shotgun builds?
A: Yes, especially with Foundry Bulwark. Shotgun pushers benefit from total armor and on-kill regen because they fight in melee range where chip damage stacks fast.

Q: Is Belstone still meta in TU22.1?
A: Yes. Tank builds remain core for raid lieutenant slots, Legendary boss tanking, and Manhunt encounters. Belstone is unchanged and still S-tier for those roles.

### Closing

Belstone Armory is the spine of every serious tank build in The Division 2. The combination of armor regen, on-kill armor, and total armor multiplier creates a survivability ceiling no other brand reaches. Keep one full set in your stash with rolls focused on Armor, Weapon Damage and Critical Hit Damage. Whether you're tanking Legendary missions, anchoring a raid, or just want to feel unkillable in Dark Hours, Belstone earns its slot every time.
