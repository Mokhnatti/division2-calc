## At a Glance

Shiny Monkey Gear is the status-effect brand. Where other skill brands focus on raw damage or repair, Shiny Monkey amplifies the duration of status effects you apply, recycles your skills faster between deploys, and adds a healthy dose of skill damage on top. For Firewall and Technician hybrid status builds, for the Foam-Memento-Shock Trap loop, and for any loadout that runs disrupts or burns as a primary mechanic, this brand is the engine.

It is also one of the most underrated DPS brands in the game. The 3-piece skill damage line is competitive with dedicated DPS brands, while the status duration and cooldown reduction stack value the others do not offer.

## Bonuses

### 1 Piece - Plus 15 Percent Status Duration

Status duration extends how long your applied status effects (burn, bleed, shock, ensnare, blind, disrupt, disorient) stay active on the target. Fifteen percent is meaningful: a base 5-second burn becomes 5.75 seconds, a 4-second shock becomes 4.6 seconds. Combined with status duration rolls on gear, the brand pushes status uptime into permanent-active territory on bosses and elites.

This bonus is build-defining for status DPS. Burns and shocks tick continuous damage; longer duration means more ticks; more ticks means higher total damage. It also extends crowd control durations - useful in raid mechanics and Heroic CP defenses.

### 2 Piece - Plus 20 Percent Skill Cooldown

Skill cooldown reduction directly cuts the time between skill deploys. Twenty percent on the 2-piece is one of the largest single-source CDR bonuses available. For status builds where the skill itself is the application source (foam, fire turret, shock trap, banshee), faster cooldown means faster reapplication and more total status applications per fight.

It also stacks with skill haste rolls and Hardwired's deploy-on-hit reduction, allowing builds to push toward near-permanent skill uptime.

### 3 Piece - Plus 18 Percent Skill Damage

The damage line. Eighteen percent skill damage is slightly below China Light Industries' 2-piece (20 percent), but it comes alongside status duration and cooldown rather than being the headline stat. For hybrid status-and-damage builds, this is exactly the right balance: enough damage to make the status meaningful, while the duration and cooldown lines amplify uptime.

The 3-piece also amplifies skill-based status damage specifically (status DoT scales with skill damage), so the bonus works double-duty on burn and shock applications.

## Best Use

Shiny Monkey Gear is built for status DPS and hybrid skill builds. Specifically:

- **Firewall status burn** - fire turret, incendiary grenade, flame chem launcher all hit harder for longer
- **Technician shock specialist** - shock trap, pulse, and shock damage builds amplified across all three lines
- **Foundry Bulwark with Memento** - Memento status uptime extended, cooldown loops tightened
- **Foam crowd control** - longer ensnare on bosses
- **Hybrid skill-DPS** - the 3-piece skill damage anchors the build, the 1- and 2-piece amplify uptime

## Item Pool

Shiny Monkey drops in all six slots and is in the standard targeted loot rotation. No exotics tied to the brand, but several named pieces with elevated rolls exist.

Recommended attribute priorities:

1. **Skill tier** as primary (a 6-tier chassis maximizes skill damage and cooldown value)
2. **Status effect** roll on the secondary attribute slot (stacks with the 1-piece)
3. **Skill damage** or **skill haste** on tertiary slots

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Only brand offering all three of: status duration, skill cooldown, and skill damage in one set
- 20 percent skill cooldown 2-piece is one of the largest CDR bonuses available
- Status duration 1-piece is build-defining for any status DPS archetype
- 18 percent skill damage 3-piece is competitive with dedicated damage brands
- Drops in every gear slot for easy targeted loot farming

### Cons

- Lower raw damage ceiling than China Light Industries on pure skill damage builds
- No skill health, so deployables are vulnerable - pair with another brand for survival
- Status duration is wasted on builds that do not apply status effects
- Skill cooldown overlaps somewhat with skill haste, leading to diminishing returns at very high stack counts

## FAQ

### Does +15 percent status duration affect ALL status types?

Yes. Burn, bleed, shock, ensnare, blind, disrupt, disorient, confuse - any applied status that has a duration is extended by the 1-piece bonus.

### Is skill cooldown the same as skill haste?

Functionally similar, but applied differently. Skill haste is a multiplier on the haste pool; skill cooldown reduction (CDR) directly reduces the cooldown timer. They stack, but with diminishing returns at high values - the cooldown timer cannot go below a hard floor regardless of stacking.

### Is Shiny Monkey better than China Light for status DPS?

For status-focused builds, yes - the duration bonus is irreplaceable. For pure skill damage with no status component, China Light wins. Hybrid status-DPS builds often run three pieces of each.

### Does the brand work with Hardwired?

Excellent synergy. Hardwired's cooldown-on-hit mechanic stacks multiplicatively with the 2-piece skill cooldown bonus, allowing skill cycles short enough that deployables can be re-summoned almost continuously.

### Does +18 percent skill damage affect status DoT?

Yes. Status damage-over-time (burn ticks, bleed ticks, shock damage) scales with skill damage, so the 3-piece amplifies the actual damage of every status tick, not just the initial skill hit.

## Closing Notes

Shiny Monkey Gear is the smart pick for status builds and hybrid skill DPS. It offers the rare combination of duration, cooldown, and damage in a single brand, which means a three-piece sheet does triple work on a status loadout where every other brand has to specialize.

If you build around fire, shock, or any other status mechanic, this is your brand. The pieces drop everywhere, the bonuses pay off across the entire build, and the synergies with Firewall, Technician, Hardwired, and Memento make it one of the most versatile skill brands once you understand what it is doing. Underrated by general players, essential for status mains.
