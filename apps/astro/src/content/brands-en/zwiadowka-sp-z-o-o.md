## At a Glance

Zwiadowka Sp. z o.o. is the magazine-and-precision DPS brand. It hands you bigger mags, faster reloads, and a hefty headshot damage multiplier - the three stats that decide the practical damage output of any rifle, marksman, or LMG build. There is no weapon damage bonus, no critical hit bonus, no skill stat - just the trio that turns "feels good to shoot" into "actually deletes things".

For marksman builds in particular, this brand is in the conversation for default endgame loadout. For LMG builds, the magazine size on the 1-piece is borderline build-defining.

## Bonuses

### 1 Piece - Plus 15 Percent Magazine Size

Magazine size scales the base magazine of every weapon you pull out. On rifles, this turns a 20-round mag into 23. On the M60 LMG, 100 rounds become 115. On marksman rifles, an 8-round mag becomes 9, which is a small but very noticeable difference for sustained boss DPS.

Magazine size also pairs with talents that scale with mag (Optimist, Sadist, Strained, On Empty). On Strained in particular - which gives crit damage as a percentage of remaining mag - a larger mag means more crit damage per shot for longer.

### 2 Piece - Plus 20 Percent Reload Speed

Reload speed is the unsung hero of practical DPS. Twenty percent is a substantial chunk - on a marksman rifle that reloads in 3.5 seconds base, the new reload is closer to 2.8 seconds, which is real combat time saved every time the mag empties.

Reload speed also helps trigger reload talents (Sadist's reload condition, On Empty's full-mag reset, Allegro's swap-and-reload) and indirectly reduces the downtime where you cannot fire. For weapons with small mags (sniper rifles, marksman, shotguns), this is the bonus that defines whether the weapon feels usable in sustained fights.

### 3 Piece - Plus 20 Percent Headshot Damage

The damage line. Headshot damage is its own multiplier in the damage formula - it stacks multiplicatively with weapon damage, crit damage, and most other damage sources. Twenty percent is one of the largest single-source HSD bonuses available outside of a dedicated talent.

For marksman, sniper, and rifle builds, headshot damage is the primary damage path - more useful than crit damage in many cases because every aimed shot is already a head shot. For SMG and AR builds firing at center mass, the bonus is wasted on most shots, but the magazine and reload bonuses still pull weight.

## Best Use

Zwiadowka Sp. z o.o. is built for precision DPS. Specifically:

- **Marksman / sniper builds** - HSD bonus directly amplifies the primary damage path
- **Rifle DPS** - especially with talents that reward sustained headshots (Optimist, Strained, Boomerang)
- **LMG builds** - the magazine size on the 1-piece extends the LMG's already-large mag for ultra-long damage windows
- **Hybrid AR builds** - reload speed and mag size still help even when most shots are body shots

## Item Pool

Zwiadowka drops across all six gear slots and rotates through standard targeted loot. Several named variants exist with elevated rolls. There are no exotics tied to the brand.

Recommended attribute priorities:

1. **Weapon damage** as the primary attribute (red cores)
2. **Headshot damage** on chest and backpack (stacks with the 3-piece)
3. **Critical hit chance** or **critical hit damage** to round out the damage profile

## Builds That Use Zwiadowka

### Marksman Headshot Specialist

- 3 pieces Zwiadowka
- 2 pieces a complementary DPS brand (Belstone Armory for crit, Wyvern Wear for stability)
- 1 piece any flexible slot
- Marksman rifle with Boomerang or Optimist talent, scope, suppressor
- Result: every headshot multiplied by the 3-piece HSD bonus, mag and reload keeping the cycle tight

### Strained Rifle DPS

- 3 pieces Zwiadowka (the 1-piece mag size pairs perfectly with Strained's reload-condition crit)
- 3 pieces Belstone Armory (crit chance plus crit damage)
- Police M4 or AK-M with Strained holster talent
- Result: a rifle that holds full crit damage from the larger mag for a longer effective window

### LMG Burn-Down

- 3 pieces Zwiadowka
- 2 pieces Petrov Defense Group (rate of fire)
- 1 piece any flexible slot
- M60 or RPK with Allegro or Optimist
- Result: 115-round mag that rarely needs to reload, and when it does, reloads quickly

### Hybrid AR DPS

- 2 pieces Zwiadowka (the 1-piece mag and 2-piece reload only)
- 4 pieces a complementary brand (Wyvern Wear for stability, or 4 pieces Striker for stack scaling)
- AR with Optimist talent, eye-of-storm or other crit-focused build
- The mag and reload combine to keep DPS smooth on a rapid-fire weapon

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Three of the most universally useful weapon DPS stats in one brand
- 20 percent headshot damage 3-piece is a top-tier HSD source for marksman builds
- Magazine size 1-piece directly enables Strained, On Empty, and other mag-based talents
- Reload speed 2-piece improves quality of life on every weapon class
- Drops in every gear slot for easy farming

### Cons

- Zero defensive stats - no armor, no hazard protection, no skill health
- No flat weapon damage or critical hit bonus, so it must be paired with a brand that provides those
- Headshot damage is wasted on hip-fire SMG builds and on AR builds that target body shots
- Brand value drops on hybrid skill-weapon builds that lean heavily on the skill side

## FAQ

### Does +15 percent magazine size affect rounded-down weapons (sniper rifles)?

Yes, but with the standard rounding rule. A 5-round mag becomes 5 rounds (15 percent of 5 is 0.75, rounds down). A 6-round mag becomes 6 (15 percent of 6 is 0.9). An 8-round mag becomes 9. The bonus is most efficient on weapons with a base mag of 7 or more.

### Is the 3-piece +20 percent HSD additive or multiplicative with HSD rolls on gear?

Additive within the headshot damage multiplier pool, like other HSD sources. The pool is then applied multiplicatively against base damage. Stacking the 3-piece with two HSD rolls on chest and backpack pushes the total HSD modifier into the 80-100 percent range, which compounds against weapon damage and crit damage.

### Is Zwiadowka better than Belstone Armory for marksman builds?

Different focuses. Belstone gives critical hit stats; Zwiadowka gives headshot damage. For marksman, every shot is intended to be a headshot, so HSD usually outvalues crit. The strongest marksman builds run both - three pieces Zwiadowka, three pieces Belstone, with Strained or Boomerang as the talent.

### Does +20 percent reload speed stack with reload speed rolls on gear?

Yes, additively, in the standard reload speed pool. There is a soft cap on reload speed, so very heavy stacking shows diminishing returns; the brand 2-piece alone is comfortably below that cap.

### Is the brand useful in DZ or PvP?

For DZ, yes - longer mags and faster reloads matter in extended firefights. For pure PvP, headshot damage remains valuable, though weapon damage and armor stats are usually prioritized first.

## Closing Notes

Zwiadowka Sp. z o.o. is the practical-DPS brand. There is no fancy interaction or hidden mechanic - it just gives you a bigger mag, a faster reload, and more damage on the head shot. For marksman, sniper, and rifle builds especially, it is one of the cleanest brands in the pool, because every line directly improves the primary damage path.

If you build precision DPS, this brand is going to be in your loadout, full stop. The only question is whether you run three pieces or six - and the answer depends on what your second brand brings to the table.
