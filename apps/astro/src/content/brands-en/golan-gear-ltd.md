## Golan Gear Ltd - Status Resist & Tank Hybrid Brand

Last reviewed: 2026-04-25 · TU22.1 · Verified vs in-game

Golan Gear Ltd is the under-the-radar brand for hybrid tank builds that need status mitigation. Its bonuses don't scream meta on paper, but they layer cleanly into Heroic+ survivability stacks where status DOTs and chip damage are the main killers. If you've been melted by burn, bleed, shock or poison in Legendary content, Golan is the brand you didn't know you needed.

### At a Glance

- 1pc: +10% Negative Effects
- 2pc: +1.5% Armor Regeneration
- 3pc: +10% Total Armor
- Role: Status Resistance / Tank hybrid
- Tier: B+ for general use, A for Legendary survivability
- Best slot count: 2pc or 3pc
- Synergies: Walker Harris & Co., Belstone, Yaahl, Murakami, Hard Wired

The brand identity is "stay alive against status." The 1pc Negative Effects (interpreted as Status Effect resistance / negative status reduction in TU22.1's effective stat model) reduces incoming status damage and duration, the 2pc Armor Regen provides constant recovery, and the 3pc Total Armor multiplies your pool. Together they create a layered defense against the chip-damage profile that defines high-difficulty content.

### Bonuses Detail

The 1pc +10% Negative Effects modifies the magnitude and duration of negative effects applied to you. In practice, this reduces incoming status duration and tick damage from enemy DOTs - bleed, burn, poison, shock, blind. In Legendary missions where Black Tusk grenadiers and Cleaners stack hazards aggressively, this layer is the difference between dying to chip damage and surviving a push.

The 2pc +1.5% Armor Regeneration is small per slot but stacks. Combined with Belstone 1pc (+1%) and Walker Harris & Co. armor regen rolls, you build to a sustainable regen tier. 1.5% of a 1.5 million armor pool is 22,500 per second, enough to mitigate incoming chip from most non-elite enemies.

The 3pc +10% Total Armor is half of Belstone's 3pc but still meaningful. It multiplies your final armor pool, scaling with chest, gloves and kneepads armor rolls. For builds that want both armor and status resist without committing to 3pc Belstone, Golan 3pc is the cleanest pickup.

### Best Use Cases

Golan shines in three archetypes:

1. Walker Harris & Co. armor regen tank. Walker provides +10% damage to elites and bonus armor regen. Golan 3pc adds total armor and another regen layer. The 1pc status resist covers the vulnerability that armor-regen builds have to DOT pressure.
2. Legendary mission survival. Legendary chip damage is the primary killer. Golan's status resist + armor regen + total armor stack create the consistency to survive long enemy waves.
3. Hybrid Belstone-Golan tank. 3pc Belstone + 2pc Golan = high armor + on-kill armor + status resist + regen. Strong for Foundry Bulwark or Heartbreaker burn tank.

It is not the right pick for pure DPS, skill builds, or solo speedruns where damage output matters more than survival.

### Items in Brand

Golan drops the standard six gear slots: mask, backpack, chest, gloves, holster, kneepads. Notable rolls per slot:

- Mask: Hazard Protection, Critical Hit Chance, Armor
- Backpack: Weapon Damage, Critical Hit Damage, Armor
- Chest: Armor, Weapon Damage, Hazard Protection
- Gloves: Weapon Damage, Critical Hit Chance, Armor
- Holster: Critical Hit Damage, Armor, Weapon Damage
- Kneepads: Armor, Hazard Protection, Weapon Damage

The Hazard Protection rolls on mask, chest and kneepads are the brand's strongest tank-utility statlines.

### Pros & Cons

Pros:
- Only brand with explicit Negative Effects bonus
- Three layered defensive bonuses (status, regen, armor)
- Hazard Protection rolls on multiple slots
- Pairs with every tank set (Walker, Belstone, Foundry, Heartbreaker)

Cons:
- 1pc Negative Effects benefit is hard to feel without high-difficulty content
- Smaller armor multipliers than Belstone
- No skill, weapon, or crit scaling
- Roll table is defense-focused, limiting offense flexibility

### FAQ

Q: What does +10% Negative Effects actually do?
A: It modifies negative status effects applied to you - reducing the duration and magnitude of bleed, burn, poison, shock, and blind. In TU22.1, the effective stat behaves as status resistance for incoming effects.

Q: Is Golan worth running over Belstone?
A: For pure armor stacking, Belstone is stronger. For builds that struggle with status DOTs in Legendary content, Golan adds the missing layer. Best results come from running both.

Q: Does +1.5% Armor Regen stack with mod rolls?
A: Yes. Brand armor regen and mod armor regen stack additively into your total regen-per-second pool.

Q: Should I run Golan 2pc or 3pc?
A: 2pc gives status resist + regen for minimal commitment. 3pc adds the total armor multiplier - worth it in Legendary or raid tank slots, optional in Heroic.

Q: Is Golan good in PvP?
A: Marginally. PvP scaling reduces brand bonuses, and Belstone or Walker are stronger raw armor sources. Golan's status resist has niche value against DOT-heavy PvP loadouts.

### Closing

Golan Gear Ltd is the unsung hero of layered tank builds. The combination of status resist, armor regen, and total armor doesn't dominate any single archetype, but it's the brand that turns "mostly survivable" into "actually survivable" in high-difficulty content. Keep at least one full set in your stash with Armor and Hazard Protection rolls, and pair with Walker Harris, Belstone or Foundry depending on your tank flavor. When Legendary chip damage is the killer, Golan is the answer.
