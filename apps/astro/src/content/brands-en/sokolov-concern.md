## At a Glance

Sokolov Concern is the Russian half of the close-quarters DPS conversation in The Division 2. Where Western brands like Walker and Česká Výroba chase generalist crit profiles, Sokolov narrows its identity hard onto two weapon families: SMGs and pistols. Every tier of the brand bonus chain reinforces that focus, which makes Sokolov one of the most specialized DPS brands in TU22.1, and one of the most punishing if you slot it on the wrong build.

The brand bonuses read as follows. One piece grants +10% SMG Damage, two pieces add +10% Pistol Damage, and three pieces stack +15% Critical Hit Chance. The first two tiers are weapon-family-specific damage multipliers, and the third tier is a generalist crit ceiling pushup that synergizes with both. If you are running an SMG primary with a pistol secondary, Sokolov's three-piece is the highest pure-DPS brand choice in the game for that archetype.

The brand pairs canonically with the Heartbreaker chest, which converts SMG hit-streak stacks into massive damage and crit windows. Heartbreaker plus three-piece Sokolov is one of the oldest meta combinations in the game and remains relevant in TU22.1.

## Bonuses Detail

### 1-Piece: +10% SMG Damage

SMG Damage is a weapon-family multiplier that applies on top of Weapon Damage and weapon-type multipliers in the final formula. A flat +10% is the single largest weapon-family bonus available at the one-piece tier in TU22.1. For any SMG main build, slotting one piece of Sokolov is among the highest-value single-slot decisions in the entire build crafting meta.

The bonus applies to all SMG variants, including exotic SMGs like Chatterbox, Reformation and Heartbreaker's preferred weapon Lefty. The flat percentage means low-base-damage rapid-fire SMGs benefit slightly more in raw DPS terms than high-base-damage burst SMGs, but both archetypes gain meaningful damage uplift.

### 2-Piece: +10% Pistol Damage

Pistol Damage is a niche stat in PvE, where pistols are typically used as emergency-reload backups or for specific exotic synergies. The two-piece tier of Sokolov rewards builds that genuinely use their secondary weapon, which is most relevant for the D50 exotic pistol, the Liberty exotic pistol, and the Regulus exotic revolver.

For non-exotic-pistol builds, the +10% Pistol Damage tier is functionally a soft tier — players slot two pieces primarily to unlock the path to the three-piece bonus, treating the pistol bonus as a bonus rather than a primary scaling layer. That said, the rise of pistol-focused niche builds in TU22.1 has made this tier more relevant than it was in earlier patches.

### 3-Piece: +15% Critical Hit Chance

Fifteen points of CHC at the three-piece tier is the highest CHC three-piece bonus in any brand in TU22.1. For SMG builds in particular, this is transformative. SMGs already lean heavily on critical hit scaling because their per-shot damage is low and their fire rate is high, so every point of CHC translates directly into more damage per second.

The three-piece bonus brings most SMG builds to or near the 60% CHC soft cap without requiring CHC core attributes elsewhere on the build, which frees up core slots for Weapon Damage or Headshot Damage rolls.

## Best Use Cases

Sokolov Concern is built for three archetypes. First, classic Heartbreaker SMG builds, where the brand's SMG Damage and CHC bonuses stack with Heartbreaker's hit-streak mechanic for one of the highest sustained close-range DPS profiles in the game. Second, dedicated pistol builds running Regulus, Liberty or D50 as the primary damage source. Third, hybrid SMG+pistol builds that swap weapons mid-engagement for sustained damage uptime.

The brand is largely useless on AR, rifle, MMR, LMG and shotgun builds. Slotting Sokolov on a non-SMG, non-pistol build wastes the first two tiers entirely and leaves only the three-piece CHC bonus pulling weight, which is not competitive with Walker's three-piece +20% CHD or Česká Výroba's three-piece +15% CHD plus Equalizer access.

## Items in Brand

Sokolov Concern covers all six gear slots: mask, backpack, chest, gloves, holster and kneepads. Stat pools across the brand favor close-range engagement, with high CHC and CHD attribute rolls. Weapon Damage and Headshot Damage are also well-represented in the secondary roll pool.

### Heartbreaker (Chest, In Sync Talent)

The Heartbreaker chest is a chest-slot named piece with the In Sync talent fixed. In Sync stacks SMG hit-streaks into massive damage and crit-chance windows, and the synergy with Sokolov's brand bonuses is the foundational pairing for the entire Heartbreaker build archetype.

### Sokolov Composure Mask

Mask-slot named with the Composure talent, restoring CHC in cover engagements.

### Sokolov Vigilance Backpack

Backpack-slot named with the Vigilance talent, +25% Weapon Damage at full health.

## Top Builds

### Heartbreaker SMG Crit Cap

Heartbreaker chest, three-piece Sokolov including a Heartbreaker chest as one of the three, two-piece Walker for the Weapon Damage and CHC, one-piece Providence for Headshot Damage. Specialization: Gunner or Survivalist. Weapon: any meta SMG, with Lefty as the canonical pairing. This build hits the CHC soft cap, sustains the Heartbreaker hit-streak through the Sokolov SMG Damage layer, and produces some of the highest sustained DPS numbers in TU22.1.

### Regulus Headshot

Three-piece Sokolov, two-piece Providence for Headshot Damage, one-piece Walker for the +5% Weapon Damage flex. Regulus exotic revolver. The Pistol Damage two-piece pulls real weight on this build because Regulus is a damage-per-shot weapon that benefits from every layer of damage scaling.

### D50 Mantis Hybrid

Three-piece Sokolov, two-piece Česká Výroba, one-piece Walker. D50 secondary, Mantis MMR primary. The Pistol Damage tier carries the D50 burst windows; the CHC three-piece pushes the Mantis crit cap. Hybrid playstyle build that punishes both close and long range.

## FAQ

### Is Sokolov Concern better than Walker for SMG builds?

Yes. Sokolov's first two tiers are SMG-family-specific damage multipliers, which Walker cannot match. For pure SMG output, three-piece Sokolov plus two-piece Walker is the canonical stack.

### Does the +10% SMG Damage stack with Heartbreaker's bonus?

Yes. Sokolov's brand bonuses are additive within the SMG Damage layer, and Heartbreaker's In Sync talent operates on a different multiplier layer. They stack cleanly.

### Can I run Sokolov on an AR build for the three-piece CHC?

You can, but you should not. The first two tiers contribute nothing to an AR build, and the three-piece CHC alone is not competitive with Walker's three-piece +20% CHD or Česká Výroba's three-piece bonus chain.

### How does Sokolov interact with the Lefty exotic pistol?

Lefty is technically a pistol class, so it benefits from both the +10% Pistol Damage two-piece and the +15% CHC three-piece. The combination is one of the strongest pistol setups in TU22.1.

### Is Heartbreaker still meta in TU22.1?

Yes. The build has been in the meta for several patches and remains a top-tier choice for close-range PvE content and Conflict PvP. The Sokolov three-piece is what makes it work.

### Does Sokolov work for shotgun builds?

No. Shotguns are a separate weapon family and do not benefit from SMG or Pistol Damage bonuses.
