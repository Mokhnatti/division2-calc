## Hana-U Corporation - Skill Damage & Repair Brand

Last reviewed: 2026-04-25 · TU22.1 · Verified vs in-game

Hana-U Corporation is the most consistent skill brand in The Division 2. Every bonus on every slot pushes skill performance higher, and unlike Empress or Electrique, Hana-U doesn't split focus between status and damage - it's all about making your skills bigger, faster, and more durable. If you run Striker drone, Sniper turret, Firestarter, Stinger Hive, or any active-damage skill, Hana-U is your foundation.

### At a Glance

- 1pc: +10% Skill Haste
- 2pc: +10% Skill Repair
- 3pc: +18% Skill Damage
- Role: Skill DPS
- Tier: S for skill builds, A for hybrid skill-tank
- Best slot count: 3pc
- Synergies: Hard Wired, Future Initiative, Murakami, Pyromaniac, Tip of the Spear, Capacitor

The brand identity is pure skill scaling. The 3pc 18% skill damage bonus is one of the largest skill damage modifiers in the game, and the 1pc skill haste plus 2pc skill repair create a complete skill-uptime package without locking you into a specific archetype.

### Bonuses Detail

The 1pc +10% Skill Haste reduces skill cooldowns. Skill Haste is multiplicative with cooldown reduction stats and brand bonuses, which means stacking 3pc Hana-U with Yaahl 3pc creates near-permanent skill uptime. For Striker drone, that's continuous fire. For Reviver Hive, that's a teammate revive every 8-10 seconds.

The 2pc +10% Skill Repair increases the rate at which skills repair themselves and the amount they recover. Restorer drone heals teammates faster, Striker drone repairs its own armor mid-fight, and turrets last longer in heavy fire. Skill Repair is the survival stat for skill-based gameplay because it keeps your active skills alive instead of forcing constant recasts.

The 3pc +18% Skill Damage is the headline. 18% is one of the highest skill damage bonuses from any brand. It scales every active-damage skill: Striker drone bullets, Sniper turret rounds, Firestarter chem ignition damage, Stinger Hive stings, Achilles seeker explosions, Thermal Bomb damage, and the new Capacitor charged shots.

### Best Use Cases

Hana-U shines in four archetypes:

1. Hard Wired skill-DPS. Hard Wired set bonus refunds skill cooldown on damage, and Hana-U adds the damage and haste to make that loop hum. Standard split: 4pc Hard Wired + 2pc Hana-U or 3+3.
2. Future Initiative healer-DPS. Future Initiative buffs allies, Hana-U scales the heals and damage of your skills. Pair Restorer drone + Striker drone for support and offense.
3. Pyromaniac fire skill build. Pyromaniac specialization combines with Firestarter chem and Hana-U skill damage for one of the highest burn DPS profiles.
4. Tip of the Spear marksman + skill hybrid. Specialization-driven skill damage on shots fired pairs with Hana-U scaling for support DPS.

It is not the right pick for weapon DPS, status pressure, or pure tank builds. Those want Petrov, Empress, or Belstone.

### Items in Brand

Hana-U drops the standard six gear slots: mask, backpack, chest, gloves, holster, kneepads. Notable rolls per slot:

- Mask: Skill Haste, Skill Damage, Status Effects
- Backpack: Skill Damage, Skill Haste, Skill Repair
- Chest: Skill Damage, Armor, Weapon Damage
- Gloves: Skill Damage, Skill Haste, Status Effects
- Holster: Skill Haste, Skill Repair, Skill Damage
- Kneepads: Skill Damage, Hazard Protection, Armor

Every slot rolls Skill Damage or Skill Haste as a primary, which makes Hana-U the easiest skill brand to farm for clean rolls.

### Pros & Cons

Pros:
- Largest brand-based Skill Damage bonus (18% on 3pc)
- Every bonus matters in any skill build
- Roll table is skill-focused on every slot
- Synergizes with all skill-tier sets and exotic skill chests

Cons:
- Zero weapon, status (status duration), or armor scaling
- 1pc Skill Haste alone is small without supporting sources
- Highly contested in the meta - rolls are competitive
- Useless in pure DPS or pure tank loadouts

### FAQ

Q: Is Hana-U better than Empress for skill builds?
A: Yes for pure skill damage. Hana-U's 3pc gives +18% Skill Damage versus Empress's 2pc 10% Skill Damage. Empress wins for status hybrid; Hana-U wins for direct damage.

Q: Does +10% Skill Repair affect Restorer drone heal speed?
A: Yes. Skill Repair scales the repair rate of all skills, including the rate at which Restorer drone heals allies and itself.

Q: Should I run 3pc Hana-U or 4pc Hard Wired?
A: They're complementary. The standard skill DPS split is 4pc Hard Wired + 2pc Hana-U for chest/backpack/gloves/kneepads + gloves/holster overlap, or 3+3 if you prefer. Both work.

Q: Does Skill Damage stack with skill mods?
A: Yes. Skill mods add their own multipliers (damage, duration, range) and stack with brand Skill Damage multiplicatively.

Q: Is Hana-U good in Heroic and Legendary?
A: Yes. Skill builds remain strong in endgame, and Hana-U's 18% skill damage is one of the most impactful single bonuses for skill survivability and lethality at high difficulty.

### Closing

Hana-U Corporation is the spine of every serious skill build in The Division 2. The combination of skill haste, skill repair, and 18% skill damage covers all three core skill stats, and the brand pairs naturally with Hard Wired, Future Initiative, Murakami, and every skill-tier exotic. Keep one full set rolled with Skill Damage on chest, backpack and gloves, Skill Haste on mask and holster, and your choice of Status Effects or Hazard Protection on the secondary slots. Every skill build starts here.
