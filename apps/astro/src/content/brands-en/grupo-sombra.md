## At a Glance

Grupo Sombra S.A. is the brand that quietly carries half the DPS meta. While Providence Defense gets the spotlight for sniper builds, Grupo Sombra gives you something arguably more universal: raw crit damage and crit chance without the headshot dependency. If you build a body-shot AR, an LMG bullet hose, or anything else where every shot matters and not just the headshots, Sombra is the brand you want.

The 2-piece bonus is the wildcard. +25% Pistol Damage seems weird on a brand built around crit, but it's there for a reason: the Liberty exotic pistol, finisher pistol talents, and pistol-swap meta builds. For everyone else, the 1pc and 3pc bonuses alone justify three slots in the brand.

| Slot | Bonus | What it does |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1 piece | +13% Critical Hit Damage | One of the highest CHD single-piece bonuses in the game |
| 2 pieces | +25% Pistol Damage | Pistol synergy for swap/finisher/Liberty builds |
| 3 pieces | +10% Critical Hit Chance | Pushes you toward CHC cap with no rolls needed |

Grupo Sombra is functionally the "anti-Providence" - same offensive role, different application. Where Providence demands headshots, Sombra rewards every crit, regardless of where it lands. For players who don't have the aim or the patience for full sniper play, Sombra is often the better DPS brand.

## Bonuses Detail

### 1-Piece: +13% Critical Hit Damage

13% CHD on a single piece is huge. To put it in perspective: most crit damage attributes on individual gear pieces roll between 8% and 12%. The 1pc Sombra bonus is essentially a "free" CHD attribute, letting your actual rolled stats focus on weapon damage, damage to armor, or other multipliers.

If you only run 1 piece of Sombra (the cherry pick), you get this 13% CHD as a flat bonus and free up 2 other slots for whatever brand you're stacking. It's one of the most efficient single-piece dips in the game.

### 2-Piece: +25% Pistol Damage

This is the brand's identity bonus. 25% pistol damage on a 2pc brand is staggering, and it exists for very specific build archetypes:

- **Liberty Exotic Pistol Builds** - pair with Memento, Determined chest, and Sombra 2pc for one-shot pistol finishers
- **Pistol Swap Meta** - empty mag, swap to pistol, hit 25% buff for cleanup
- **Glass Cannon Pistol Builders** (rare but real)

For non-pistol builds, this 2pc is wasted. That's fine - the brand is still strong at 1pc or 3pc and you skip the 2pc tier.

### 3-Piece: +10% Critical Hit Chance

10% CHC on a brand bonus is enormous. The 60% soft cap is achievable with just a few attribute rolls when you have 10% from the brand, which means your remaining attribute slots can stack CHD instead of CHC. This is what makes 3pc Sombra so efficient: the brand itself solves your CHC cap problem.

## Best Use Cases

- **Body-Shot AR DPS:** the canonical use. 3pc Sombra + Memento + Determined chest + ACR-E or P416. Heroic-clearing AR DPS without sniper precision requirements.
- **LMG Builds:** Stoner LMG, Black Tusk LMG, and L86 LSW all benefit from 3pc Sombra. LMGs don't headshot consistently, so Sombra outperforms Providence here.
- **Liberty Pistol Finisher:** 3pc Sombra (with the 2pc bonus active) + Liberty + Memento for pistol one-shots on red bars and elites.
- **Hybrid Without Skill Focus:** when you want CHC and CHD without dedicating gear sets to it, 3pc Sombra delivers. Pair with Wyvern Wear 2pc for hybrid Skill Damage + crit.

Grupo Sombra is *not* great for skill builds, sniper one-shots (use Providence), or tank builds. It's a pure crit-DPS brand and shines wherever sustained crit damage matters more than headshot multipliers.

## Items in Brand

Grupo Sombra S.A. has a notable named piece pool, with the brand often appearing in targeted loot rotations across Warlords of New York and DC zones. Here are the highlights:

### Named Pieces in Grupo Sombra

The brand pool includes named gear with the standard Sombra brand bonus plus exotic-tier talents on the named slot. While the exact named roster varies by item-pool patches, Grupo Sombra consistently appears in:

- **Holster slot** - one of the most reliable Sombra targeted-loot slots
- **Kneepads slot** - crit-focused rolls with Sombra branding
- **Gloves slot** - particularly desirable for crit-damage attribute rolls

### Recommended Slot Distribution

For a 3pc Grupo Sombra DPS build:
- **Gloves + Holster + Kneepads:** classic distribution. Keeps mask, chest, and backpack open for Coyote's, Walker Harris Determined, and Memento.
- **Mask + Gloves + Kneepads:** alternative if you want a named holster from another brand (Petrov BSG, Hana-U).

The flexibility of slot placement makes Sombra easy to combine with named pieces from any other brand, which is a big part of why it stays in the meta across so many builds.

## Top Builds Using This Brand

### Classic AR DPS (PvE Heroic)

- 3pc Grupo Sombra (Gloves, Holster, Kneepads)
- 1x Coyote's Mask (named, +15% CHC vs flanked)
- 1x Walker Harris Determined chest (Vigilance talent)
- 1x Memento backpack
- ACR-E or P416 with Optimist or Vindictive
- Result: 60% CHC cap easily reached, CHD stacked through Memento + Sombra + Determined, sustained AR damage clears Heroic content reliably.

### LMG Bullet Hose (Solo / Group Heroic)

- 3pc Grupo Sombra
- 1x Coyote's Mask
- 1x Memento or Walker Harris chest
- 1x Stoner LMG with Vindictive or Sadist
- LMGs don't reload. Stack damage and never stop firing. The 3pc CHC bonus matters most here because LMG fire rate means you crit constantly.

### Liberty Pistol Finisher (Specialty)

- 3pc Grupo Sombra (2pc active, +25% pistol damage)
- 1x Memento
- 1x Determined chest
- Liberty exotic pistol
- Primary AR or LMG of choice
- Empty primary mag, swap to Liberty, finish with pistol crits boosted by 25%. Niche but devastating.

### Hybrid Crit + Skill (PvE Heroic)

- 3pc Grupo Sombra
- 2pc Wyvern Wear (mask + gloves) - status amplification
- 1x Memento
- AR + Striker drone
- Goal: stack crit damage from Sombra, status amplification from Wyvern, and sustained DPS from AR + drone combo.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros

- 13% CHD on 1pc is among the highest single-piece CHD bonuses in the game
- 10% CHC on 3pc solves your CHC-cap problem with no rolled attributes
- Universally useful across AR, LMG, SMG, and pistol builds
- Pistol 2pc bonus is best-in-slot for Liberty/finisher builds
- Plays well with Memento, Determined chest, and Vigilance/Vindictive talents
- Flexible slot distribution (gloves, holster, kneepads, mask all work)
- Stays meta across multiple TU patches

### Cons

- Zero skill synergy (skip for skill builds)
- 2pc Pistol Damage is wasted on non-pistol builds
- No defensive bonuses
- Less optimal than Providence for sniper one-shots
- Targeted loot can be slow depending on rotation week

## FAQ

**Q: Is Grupo Sombra better than Providence Defense?**
A: Depends on your build. Providence wins for sniper headshots. Sombra wins for body-shot ARs, LMGs, and any build where you can't or don't want to rely on headshots.

**Q: Should I run 2pc Sombra for the pistol bonus on a non-pistol build?**
A: No. If you're not running Liberty or a pistol-finisher build, skip the 2pc. Take 1pc for the CHD or commit to 3pc for the full crit triangle.

**Q: Does the 1pc CHD stack with rolled CHD attributes?**
A: Yes. All CHD in The Division 2 stacks additively into a single pool. The 1pc adds 13% to whatever you've rolled.

**Q: Can I run Sombra on an MMR sniper build?**
A: You can, but Providence outperforms it because of the 13% HSD on 1pc. Sombra is for builds where headshots aren't the main damage source.

**Q: What's the best chest talent to pair with 3pc Sombra?**
A: Vigilance for sustained DPS, Obliterate for armor-strip burst, or Glass Cannon for Legendary content with a coordinated group.

**Q: Is Sombra still meta in TU22.1?**
A: Yes. The 1pc CHD and 3pc CHC bonuses are core to most non-sniper DPS builds and have remained unchanged through balance passes.

## Closing

Grupo Sombra S.A. is the brand for players who don't want to perfectly aim every shot. It rewards consistency over precision, sustained DPS over burst, and gives you both crit chance and crit damage in three gear slots without forcing you to hit heads. For body-shot AR builds, LMG bullet hoses, and anyone who wants meta DPS without sniper-level micro, Sombra is the foundation.

The 2pc pistol bonus is a love letter to Liberty mains and finisher-build players, but the brand's real strength is the 1pc/3pc combo: 13% CHD and 10% CHC for nothing more than committing to three brand slots. That's an offer no DPS builder should turn down.
