## At a Glance

Česká Výroba s.r.o. is one of the most respected DPS brands in The Division 2, and after the TU22.1 economy pass it remains a cornerstone for crit-stacking red builds. The brand identity is unmistakable: Czech precision engineering wrapped around a three-piece bonus chain that funnels every roll into raw damage output. Where many DPS brands force a compromise between sustain and burst, Česká Výroba leans hard into burst with magazine economy bolted on as a quality-of-life uplift.

The bonus structure reads as follows. One piece grants +8% Critical Hit Chance, two pieces add +20% Magazine Size, and three pieces stack +15% Critical Hit Damage on top. Each tier is meaningful on its own, which means players are not punished for splitting two pieces across a hybrid build. That flexibility is rare in the current sandbox and a major reason Česká Výroba shows up in nearly every endgame DPS guide.

The brand also carries one of the most coveted named pieces in the game: the Equalizer chest, which rolls the Obliterate talent. Obliterate scales headshot damage the longer you keep landing critical hits, and its synergy with the in-brand crit bonuses is the textbook example of vertical scaling in build crafting.

## Bonuses Detail

### 1-Piece: +8% Critical Hit Chance

Critical Hit Chance is the gateway stat for every red build in TU22.1. The soft cap remains at 60% and Česká Výroba's eight points are roughly equivalent to a high-rolled CHC core attribute. Slotting a single Česká Výroba piece into a five-piece build is one of the most efficient ways to free up a core slot for Weapon Damage or Headshot Damage rolls elsewhere.

In practical terms, a player running Providence backpack, Walker chest, gloves and holster from Walker, plus a single Česká Výroba kneepad, sees a flat eight-point CHC swing without sacrificing any other brand bonus. That is the cleanest stat-flex in the game.

### 2-Piece: +20% Magazine Size

Magazine Size is the unsung hero of sustained DPS. The +20% bonus rounds in the player's favor on every legal weapon family, which means a 30-round AR becomes 36 rounds, a 50-round LMG becomes 60, and a 21-round MMR becomes 25. Reload uptime is the silent killer of theoretical DPS numbers, and twenty percent more rounds before the next reload is twenty percent fewer reload-shaped holes in your damage curve.

This bonus also enables specific exotic synergies. The Bullet King LMG never reloads, so the +20% extends its overheat window. The Big Alejandro AR triples the value of every magazine bonus through its talent, turning +20% into a meaningful damage multiplier rather than just a sustain stat.

### 3-Piece: +15% Critical Hit Damage

Critical Hit Damage is multiplicative in the final damage formula, which means +15% CHD applied on top of an already-stacked CHD pool produces non-linear gains. A build sitting at 100% CHD that adds Česká Výroba's three-piece reaches 115%, but the perceived damage uplift is closer to 7-8% on actual TTK because of how the formula compounds with Weapon Damage and Headshot Damage layers.

## Best Use Cases

Česká Výroba shines in three archetypes. First, classic four-red Crit builds running Providence backpack and Česká Výroba's three-piece for headshot rifles and MMRs. Second, hybrid Obliterate builds that lean on the Equalizer chest as the centerpiece and stack one or two additional Česká Výroba slots for the CHD layer. Third, magazine-hungry exotics where the +20% Magazine Size translates directly into damage windows.

The brand is less effective on builds that do not crit. Shotgun primary builds, status-effect skill builds and tank PvE builds gain very little from the brand bonuses and should look elsewhere.

## Items in Brand

Česká Výroba covers all six gear slots: mask, backpack, chest, gloves, holster and kneepads. Each slot rolls native to the brand's identity, which means high CHC and CHD attribute pools, with Weapon Damage and Headshot Damage as common secondary rolls. The mask and kneepads tend to roll the cleanest crit cores, while the chest and backpack favor utility and damage attributes respectively.

The brand's named pieces are where Česká Výroba truly separates itself from the field.

### Equalizer (Chest, Obliterate Talent)

The Equalizer is a chest-slot named piece with the Obliterate talent fixed. Obliterate reads: "Critical hits increase Headshot Damage by 1% per stack, up to 25 stacks. Stacks decay after 5 seconds without a critical hit." That is +25% Headshot Damage on top of every other layer once the build is humming, and it stacks multiplicatively with the rest of the CHD pool.

### Determined (Backpack, Vigilance Talent)

A backpack named that pairs naturally with Obliterate for MMR builds.

### Wave-1 Czech Production Mask (Mask, Composure)

Mask-slot named with the Composure talent, restoring CHC after damage dealt while in cover.

## Top Builds

### Obliterate MMR

Equalizer chest, Determined backpack, three-piece Česká Výroba completing through gloves or kneepads, two-piece Providence on the remaining slots. Specialization: Sharpshooter. Weapon: Mantis or Nemesis. This build leverages the full brand stack, hits the CHC soft cap effortlessly and turns every MMR pull into a five-figure crit.

### Big Alejandro Magazine Stack

Three-piece Česká Výroba plus two-piece Fenris, Big Alejandro AR, Vile mask. The +20% Magazine Size doubles down with Big Alejandro's per-magazine damage scaling for one of the highest sustained DPS profiles in TU22.1.

### Heartbreaker Crit Hybrid

Heartbreaker chest, two-piece Česká Výroba (CHC + Magazine Size), three-piece Sokolov for the SMG synergy. A backup option for SMG players who want the Česká Výroba CHC layer without committing to the full three-piece.

## FAQ

### Is Česká Výroba better than Walker, Harris & Co. for crit builds?

For pure CHD ceiling, Česká Výroba edges out Walker thanks to the +15% three-piece and the Equalizer chest. Walker is more flexible for two-piece splits because both of its early tiers are pure damage stats. The honest answer is most top builds run a mix of both.

### Does the +20% Magazine Size affect grenade launchers or signature weapons?

No. Magazine Size bonuses do not apply to signature weapons or specialization grenade launchers. The bonus applies only to primary and secondary weapons.

### What is the chance of rolling Obliterate on a non-Equalizer chest?

Obliterate is exclusive to the Equalizer named piece in TU22.1. You cannot reroll a different chest into Obliterate at the recalibration station.

### Should I prioritize the Equalizer drop or grind for a perfect three-piece roll first?

Equalizer first. The chest defines the build identity. Three-piece optimization is a long-tail project once the centerpiece is locked.

### Does the brand work in PvP?

Yes, but with caveats. CHC and CHD bonuses apply normally, but Magazine Size is less impactful in PvP because most engagements end before reload windows matter. Equalizer's Obliterate talent is strong in extended skirmishes but loses value in burst-down trades.

### How does Česká Výroba compare to Petrov Defense Group?

Petrov is LMG-specialized; Česká Výroba is generalist. If you run LMGs as a primary, Petrov outperforms. For everything else, Česká Výroba is the safer pick.
