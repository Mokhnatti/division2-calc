## At a Glance

Alps Summit Armaments is the cleanest pure-skill brand in The Division 2. Three pieces, three skill stats, no filler. If your build runs deployables and you want them to repair faster, last longer, and come back sooner, this is the brand sheet you write the rest of your loadout against.

Unlike brands that hand you skill damage with one bonus and pad the others, Alps stays focused. Repair, duration, haste - the three numbers that decide whether a skill build lives or dies in actual combat. Drone tanks, turret pets, hive supports, demolitionist seekers all benefit, regardless of damage source.

## Bonuses

### 1 Piece - Plus 18 Percent Skill Repair

Skill repair scales the rate at which restorative skills heal targets and the rate at which your own skills (drone, turret, shield) repair their own armor. The 18 percent number is large for a 1-piece roll - most brands give 10 to 12 - and it shows up immediately on the chem launcher reinforcer, the restorer hive, and the fire turret's self-repair.

For solo skill players, it is a quality-of-life bonus that keeps gear like Reviver Hive and Fixer Drone topped up. For group skill supports, it is a direct healing throughput multiplier.

### 2 Piece - Plus 30 Percent Skill Duration

Duration extends the active life of skills that have one - shield uptime, decoy uptime, jammer pulse, banshee pulse, hive cycles, defender drone reflection. Thirty percent is a heavy number; on a 30-second shield it is nine free seconds before recharge. On the longest-running skills (Banshee, Decoy, Defender) it can mean the difference between two cycles per fight and three.

This is the bonus that anchors the whole set. Most skill brands skip duration because it is hard to balance. Alps gives you the largest single-source duration roll in the game.

### 3 Piece - Plus 20 Percent Skill Haste

Skill haste reduces cooldowns. At 20 percent it is one of the meatiest haste bonuses available outside of dedicated cooldown talents. Combined with duration from the 2-piece, your skills are up longer **and** come back faster - the two stats that matter most for skill uptime work hand in hand.

Stacked with skill haste rolls on chest and backpack and a perfectly-rolled mask, builds running Alps three-piece can sit at over 100 percent total haste, which folds cooldowns to roughly half their base length.

## Best Use

Alps Summit Armaments works on every skill archetype. The cleanest fits:

- **Hardwired support** - Hardwired's job is recycling skill cooldowns; Alps amplifies that loop.
- **Capacitor / Empress hybrid skill DPS** - more skill uptime equals more skill damage windows.
- **Reviver / Restorer healer** - duration extends the healing field, repair makes each cycle stronger.
- **Decoy and Banshee crowd control** - both want maximum uptime to control room flow.
- **Foundry Bulwark shield specialist** - 30 percent shield duration is enormous on a sponge shield.

## Item Pool

Alps Summit drops in all six slots, with named variants in the targeted loot rotation regularly. The brand is in the standard pool - no exotic gear pieces are tied to it - so a clean three-piece is reachable in a few hours of focused farming on a targeted loot day.

Recommended attribute priorities on Alps pieces:

1. **Skill tier** as the primary armor attribute (you want a 6-skill-tier chassis)
2. **Skill haste** on chest and backpack
3. **Skill damage** or **skill repair** on secondary slots, depending on whether you are DPS or support

## Builds That Use Alps Summit

### Hardwired Recycler

- 4 pieces Hardwired
- 2 pieces Alps Summit Armaments (the 2-piece +30 percent duration)
- Result: skills that almost never come down. Each hit on the deployed skill subtracts cooldown; Alps stretches the active window so more hits land before reset.

### Three-Piece Skill Anchor

- 3 pieces Alps Summit
- 3 pieces a damage-focused brand - Murakami for skill damage, Empress for repair, China Light for skill damage and explosive
- Six skill tiers and the most useful triple-skill stat sheet in the game

### Capacitor Skill DPS

- Capacitor backpack, Memento or any high-tier mask
- Three pieces Alps for haste/duration/repair
- Two pieces of a skill-damage brand
- Run with explosive skills (artillery turret, cluster seekers); the haste keeps the cycle tight, duration extends the artillery turret's barrage

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Three skill stats from one brand - no other brand offers this density of skill utility
- 30 percent duration is the highest single-source duration bonus available
- Universally useful on every skill archetype, support or damage
- Drops in every slot, so a clean three-piece is realistic to farm
- Pairs cleanly with both gear sets (Hardwired, Foundry, Striker drone tank) and brand-only builds

### Cons

- Zero combat / weapon damage - your weapon is purely a finisher
- No skill damage, so it is a utility brand, not a DPS amplifier - you must pair it with damage from another source
- Below par for non-skill builds; the bonuses are wasted on weapon DPS or tanks
- Brand requires a 6-skill-tier chassis to make full use of the haste; on lower-tier hybrids the value drops

## FAQ

### Does the 2-piece duration affect ALL skills, or only timed ones?

Only skills that have an active duration. Pulse, decoy, banshee, shield, hive cycles, defender drone reflection, fire turret burn time. One-shot skills (cluster seeker, artillery launch, sticky bomb) ignore the duration bonus - their effect is instant.

### How does +20 percent skill haste interact with cooldown reduction stats?

Skill haste is multiplicative with itself on most calculations - 20 percent from Alps stacks with haste rolls on gear and with talents like Calculated. The exact formula uses the standard haste-to-cooldown conversion, so 100 percent haste roughly equals a 50 percent cooldown reduction.

### Is Alps better than China Light Industries for skill DPS?

Different roles. China Light gives skill damage and skill health - it is the offense-and-survival skill brand. Alps gives uptime - it is the utility skill brand. The strongest skill builds run them together, three pieces each.

### Does +18 percent skill repair help my own armor?

Indirectly. It improves the rate at which Restorer Hive, Fixer Drone, and Reinforcer chem launcher heal you (and your team). It does not directly affect your own armor regen stat.

### Is Alps useful in group raid play?

Yes, especially for supports. Healer specs running Reviver Hive, Restorer, and Reinforcer chem all benefit from the repair bonus, while duration keeps the field alive longer between deploys. Raid bosses with long damage phases reward longer-lasting skill uptime.

## Closing Notes

Alps Summit Armaments is the most stat-dense skill brand in the game. There is no padding - every bonus is a number you actually want on a skill build. Skill repair, duration, haste: the three pillars of skill uptime, all in one brand. If you run deployables for a living, you should have a clean three-piece in your stash and a four-armor backup in case targeted loot rolls another brand week.

For anyone building from scratch as a skill main, this is where you start. The rest of the loadout fills in around what Alps does not cover - damage, survivability, mod slots.
