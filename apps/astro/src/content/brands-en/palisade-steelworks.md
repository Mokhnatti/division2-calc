## At a Glance

Palisade Steelworks is the second tier of the tank brand pool, sitting alongside Uzina Getica as a complementary defensive option. Where Uzina is pure armor, Palisade adds the second layer that pure-armor builds miss: hazard protection. Add an armor-on-kill refresh and a 20 percent total armor 3-piece, and you get a tank brand that handles fire, gas, bleed, and DoT pressure as well as plain bullet damage.

Palisade is the brand that turns a "bullet sponge" into a "any-damage sponge". For Heroic and Legendary content where status effects are the real killer, Palisade is often more useful than Uzina alone.

## Bonuses

### 1 Piece - Plus 10 Percent Armor on Kill

A free armor regen pulse on every elimination. Ten percent of total armor restored per kill, on a generous trigger window. On a 1.2 million armor build, every kill returns 120K plate. In a busy room (Summit floor, Countdown, Heroic CP), this becomes a near-continuous regen stream.

Compared to the 2-piece bonus on Uzina (10 percent on kill), Palisade gives the same effect on the very first piece, freeing up the 2-piece slot for hazard protection. The result is a brand that punches above its piece count.

### 2 Piece - Plus 20 Percent Hazard Protection

Hazard protection reduces incoming status damage and reduces the duration of status effects on the player. Twenty percent is the largest single-source haz pro bonus on any brand. It applies to:

- Bleed damage
- Burn damage (incendiary, fire turrets)
- Disrupt and stun durations
- Toxic gas DoT
- Shock damage
- Pulse-marker damage spike

For Heroic and Legendary content where rusher elites bleed you and chem launchers blanket the room in fire, this is the bonus that keeps you alive when armor cores alone would not.

### 3 Piece - Plus 20 Percent Total Armor

Identical to Uzina Getica's 3-piece line: 20 percent multiplicative armor on top of the rest of the armor stack. Combined with the 1-piece on-kill regen and the 2-piece haz pro, the 3-piece turns Palisade into a complete tanking package - you have more plate, you regen plate on kill, and you take less status damage.

## Best Use

Palisade Steelworks fits any tank archetype, with peak value on:

- **Hazard-heavy content** - Heroic Countdown, Legendary missions, raid encounters with status pressure
- **Foundry Bulwark shield builds** - hazard protection helps the player even when behind the shield
- **Heartbreaker hybrid tanks** - the on-kill armor regen feeds Heartbreaker's armor-shred-to-damage loop
- **Solo tank specialists** - Palisade's all-around defenses suit running heroic alone, where status effects compound quickly

It pairs naturally with Uzina Getica (3-piece Uzina + 3-piece Palisade is a top-tier raid tank loadout) and with Richter and Kaiser GmbH for repair-flavored tanks.

## Item Pool

Palisade Steelworks drops in all six gear slots and is included in the standard targeted loot rotation. There are no exotics tied to the brand, but several named pieces exist with elevated rolls.

Recommended attribute priorities:

1. **Armor** on the secondary attribute slot (stacks with the 3-piece)
2. **Hazard protection** to compound with the 2-piece bonus
3. **Armor regeneration** for additional sustain on top of the on-kill refresh

## Builds That Use Palisade Steelworks

### Hazard Tank (Solo Heroic)

- 3 pieces Palisade Steelworks
- 3 pieces Uzina Getica
- Result: 1.4M+ armor, on-kill regen from both brands, 20 percent hazard protection floor
- Purpose: solo Heroic content where status effects are unavoidable

### Foundry Bulwark Hazard Specialist

- 4 pieces Foundry Bulwark
- 2 pieces Palisade Steelworks (the on-kill regen and hazard protection)
- Bulwark Brace shield, six armor cores
- Result: shield uptime plus haz pro for the moments the shield is down

### Heartbreaker Status Resist

- 4 pieces Heartbreaker gear set
- 2 pieces Palisade
- The 1-piece armor on kill keeps the Heartbreaker damage buff cycling; the 2-piece haz pro covers the brand's lack of status defense
- Run with a high-fire-rate weapon to maximize armor shred

### Three-Piece Hybrid Anchor

- 3 pieces Palisade
- 3 pieces a complementary brand (Belstone for crit tanks, Petrov for ROF tanks, Richter and Kaiser for repair tanks)
- Full 3-piece bonuses unlock both the Palisade armor multiplier and the partner brand's most powerful line

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Best hazard protection bonus among brands (20 percent on the 2-piece)
- On-kill armor regen on the 1-piece - tank value from a single piece
- 20 percent total armor on the 3-piece matches Uzina Getica's headline number
- Drops in every gear slot for easy targeted loot farming
- Covers status effects that pure-armor brands ignore

### Cons

- No offensive contribution - weapon and skill damage are zero on every line
- Some redundancy with Uzina if running both - the 1-piece armor regen and the 3-piece total armor overlap thematically
- Hazard protection diminishes against high-tier elite damage but does not reach 100 percent immunity
- Like all tank brands, useless on glass cannon and red-core builds

## FAQ

### Is Palisade Steelworks better than Uzina Getica?

Different jobs. Uzina maximizes raw armor; Palisade adds the hazard protection layer Uzina lacks. For Heroic and Legendary content, Palisade is generally more useful. For raids where pure plate is king, Uzina has a slight edge. Most top tank builds use both, three pieces each.

### Does +20 percent hazard protection cap on the brand alone, or stack with rolls?

It stacks with hazard protection rolls on gear up to the 95 percent in-game soft cap. Stacking the brand 2-piece with two haz pro rolls on chest and backpack is the standard recipe for hitting the cap on a tank build.

### Does the 1-piece on-kill armor regen trigger on skill kills?

Yes - drone, turret, seeker, and hive kills all count toward the trigger.

### Does Palisade work for skill builds?

No. The brand has no skill stats and no skill health. Skill builds should use Alps Summit and China Light instead.

### How does Palisade interact with Foundry Bulwark?

The 1-piece armor on kill helps the player stay topped up while the shield is regenerating. The 2-piece haz pro covers status damage that bypasses the shield entirely (fire DoT, bleed). The combination is one of the most reliable solo Heroic tank loadouts.

## Closing Notes

Palisade Steelworks is the well-rounded tank brand. It offers the same headline armor multiplier as Uzina Getica but trades pure armor depth for hazard protection breadth. For solo tanks running difficult content where bleed, fire, and gas decide the fight, Palisade is the smarter pick.

For raid tanks and pure sponge builds, the right answer is usually "both" - three pieces of each, two complete brand bonuses, and a build that handles every type of damage the game can throw at you. Palisade is one of the two pillars of high-end tank composition, and it earns the slot through versatility.
