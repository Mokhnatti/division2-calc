## At a Glance

The AUG A3 Para XS is the bullpup-pattern submachine gun in The Division 2 — a compact, controllable SMG that trades the highest-tier RPM of its class for excellent stability and per-shot damage. With a 24,000 base damage, an 800 RPM, a 30-round magazine, a brisk 1.9-second reload, and the standard 1.5x SMG headshot multiplier, the AUG A3 Para XS sits in the upper-middle of the SMG archetype as a controlled, mid-range option.

This is the SMG you pick when you want the rifle-adjacent feel of an AR with the close-range scaling and set-bonus options that make SMGs the meta close-range pick. The bullpup form factor and the relatively slower 800 RPM keep recoil manageable without sacrificing the sustained-DPS that defines the SMG class.

- **Muzzle** — Compensator (vertical) to flatten the bullpup recoil

## Talent

The AUG A3 Para XS rolls **Frenzy** as its primary talent in most loot drops. Frenzy adds weapon damage and rate of fire after a reload from empty, which on an 800 RPM SMG with a 1.9-second reload turns into a near-permanent uptime once you commit to the playstyle. Frenzy is one of the strongest sustained-DPS talents on SMGs because of how cheap the activation cost is — every empty mag is a free buff.

Alternative rolls on the AUG A3 Para XS include **Allegro** (always-on flat damage), **Greased** (handling and damage on swap), and **In Sync** (skill-synergy bonus). For pure DPS, Frenzy is the pick. For mode-switching builds with a skill chassis, In Sync is interesting.

The bullpup's controllable recoil means you actually realize the post-reload Frenzy buff at headshot range, which is more than can be said for some of the higher-RPM SMGs that struggle to land tags during the talent window.

## Top Builds

### Hunter's Fury AUG (PvP and aggressive PvE)

Four-piece Hunter's Fury, AUG A3 Para XS in primary, shotgun or pistol secondary. The set bonus does the heavy lifting, and the AUG's high per-shot damage means each Fury-stacked round hits harder than on a 950-RPM rival. This is one of the cleanest Fury loadouts in the game.

### Striker AUG (sustained PvE)

Four-piece Striker, Sokolov chest, Walker holster, four red cores. The 800 RPM keeps Striker stacks healthy, and the Frenzy roll covers the engagement-extending reload windows. A versatile build that handles every PvE difficulty.

### Picaro's Holster AUG

Picaro's Holster + the AUG creates a high-damage, high-uptime SMG primary that benefits from the holster's unique reload bonus. Pair with a Determined chest for a forgiving sustained-DPS chassis.

## PvE vs PvP

In PvE, the AUG A3 Para XS is a top-five SMG. The high per-shot damage, controllable 800 RPM, and Frenzy talent produce excellent sustained DPS at the close-to-mid range that SMGs are built for. The 30-round magazine is generous enough that you do not feel pressured into reload cycles, and the 1.9-second reload is fast enough that even Frenzy-cycle players can keep uptime high.

In PvP, the AUG is a strong pick for aggressive close-range play. The 800 RPM is on the slower side for SMG TTK racing, but the per-shot damage compensates, and the 1.5x headshot multiplier produces a competitive TTK on tagged shots. Hunter's Fury AUG is one of the most respected close-range PvP builds in the game.

## Pros and Cons

**Pros**

- 24k base damage is on the high end of the SMG class
- 1.9 s reload is class-leading
- Frenzy is among the strongest sustained-DPS SMG talents
- Bullpup recoil is excellent, even at sustained fire
- Pairs perfectly with Hunter's Fury

**Cons**

- 800 RPM is slower than the top-tier SMGs (P90, MP7)
- 1.5x headshot multiplier is class-standard, not premium
- Optimal range is short — falls off past 20 m
- No exotic-tier ceiling

## FAQ

**Is the AUG A3 Para XS still competitive in TU22.1?** Yes. The rifle was untouched in the last balance pass and remains a top-five SMG for both PvE and PvP. Frenzy is still meta, and the bullpup recoil profile is still one of the best in the class.

**AUG A3 Para XS or P90?** The P90 has a higher RPM and a 50-round magazine, but a lower per-shot damage. For Hunter's Fury and aggressive close-range, the AUG; for sustained Striker stacks and per-magazine total damage, the P90.

**What attributes should I roll?** Weapon damage on the major, headshot damage or critical hit chance on the minor. Damage to armor is a strong tertiary for PvE.

**Is Frenzy still meta?** Yes — the talent was not touched in the last patch and remains one of the strongest sustained-DPS SMG talents in the loot pool.

## Closing

The AUG A3 Para XS is the SMG for agents who want close-range scaling without the recoil-management circus of higher-RPM rifles. The high per-shot damage, controllable bullpup recoil, and Frenzy talent produce a clean, satisfying close-range tool that slots into most aggressive PvE and PvP builds. Roll one with weapon damage and headshot damage, pair it with Hunter's Fury or Striker, and let the bullpup do the work.
