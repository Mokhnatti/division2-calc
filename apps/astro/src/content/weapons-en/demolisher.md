## At a Glance

The Demolisher is a named light machine gun built around sustained suppression and area denial — a high-RPM LMG with a 60-round belt magazine, 50,000 base damage, and a 4-second reload that punishes mid-fight reload windows but rewards full-mag commitment. The 1.55× headshot multiplier (slightly above the 1.5× standard) gives it a small but meaningful edge for headshot-focused builds, and the 850 RPM cyclic rate is among the highest in the LMG category.

In TU22.1, the Demolisher fills the "high-RPM aggressive LMG" niche. Where weapons like the Stoner LMG offer slower per-shot pacing for accuracy-focused builds, the Demolisher leans into sustained pressure — the 60-round mag and 850 RPM mean you can fully commit to a target for over 4 seconds of continuous fire without reload interruption. This makes it ideal for stack-ramping builds where you need uninterrupted fire to reach maximum stacks before the reload window forces a pause.

Theoretical sustained DPS reaches approximately 708,000 before mods, achievable in real engagements thanks to the 60-round mag. Recoil is heavy — typical LMG behaviour with strong upward kick and noticeable horizontal sway. Most LMG builds deploy a bipod or use cover-mounted fire to stabilize, and the Demolisher rewards both. The 1.55× headshot multiplier is a small but valuable bonus that compounds with crit damage stacks for meaningful per-shot uplift on tracked headshots.

The 4-second reload is the Demolisher's biggest weakness. In sustained engagements, reload exposure is dangerous — 4 seconds is long enough for an unfocused team to push your position. Reload speed mods become critical, and many Demolisher builds prioritize reload speed over damage rolls to compensate.

## Talent

The Demolisher rolls **Strained** as a common high-value talent — bonus crit damage at the cost of reduced armor. Strained synergizes with crit-focused builds where the headshot multiplier and crit damage stacking matter more than survivability. The talent is risky but powerful, especially in PvE where you can position to avoid incoming fire.

Other strong rolls include **Allegro** (improved rate of fire after kill) and **Steady Handed** (reduced recoil on sustained fire). Avoid **Eyeless** rolls (the talent's blind condition is rarely useful at endgame) and **Fast Hands** (the duration is too short to matter on a 4s reload).

## Top Builds

The Demolisher fits several archetypes:

**Strained Crit Striker:** Strained chest piece, Memento backpack, Striker brand pieces, Demolisher primary. Strained's bonus crit damage compounds with Striker stacks and the 1.55× headshot multiplier. The 60-round mag fully ramps Striker stacks, and the per-shot output on headshots reaches 100k+ on a tuned build.

**Heartbreaker Sustained DPS:** Heartbreaker chest piece thrives on sustained fire, and the Demolisher's 60-round mag and 850 RPM keep Heartbreaker proc uptime steady. The 4-second reload is offset by extended Heartbreaker damage windows.

**Negotiator's Dilemma Multi-Target:** Negotiator's gloves apply marks on hit, and the Demolisher's high RPM and large mag mean you mark multiple targets quickly during a single engagement. Excellent for group play and mob clearing on Heroic.

**Foundry Bulwark Tank-DPS Hybrid:** Foundry Bulwark increases armor regen on LMG weapon damage, and the Demolisher's sustained fire keeps regen continuous. A solid tank-DPS hybrid for solo Heroic and Legendary content.

## PvE-PvP

In PvE, the Demolisher is a top-tier LMG for stack-ramping and sustained-pressure builds. On Heroic and Legendary, it carries strong DPS thanks to its mag size, RPM, and headshot multiplier. The 4-second reload is a real liability — pair the Demolisher with a fast-swap secondary (SMG or pistol) to cover reload exposure windows. Excellent for elite chunking and crowd control.

In PvP, the Demolisher is more situational. The high RPM and 60-round mag are competitive in sustained engagements, but the 4-second reload window is a death sentence in skilled Conflict and Dark Zone fights. Most PvP players prefer faster-reloading LMGs or AR alternatives. Where the Demolisher does shine in PvP is in defensive holds — a player camping a chokepoint with the Demolisher on overwatch can deny advancement effectively.

## FAQ

**Where does the Demolisher drop?**
The Demolisher is a named LMG drop from heroic+ content with rotating drop pools. Check current week's named drop list for specifics — TU22.1 has it in Black Tusk territory rotations.

**Is the Demolisher better than the Stoner LMG?**
For sustained DPS and stack-ramping, yes — the 850 RPM and 60-round mag outpace the Stoner. For accuracy and per-shot precision, the Stoner has the edge. Pick based on build style.

**Does the 1.55× headshot multiplier matter?**
Yes, meaningfully. Compared to the standard 1.5× multiplier, the 1.55× adds roughly 3.3% to all headshot damage, which compounds with crit damage stacking for noticeable per-shot uplift on tuned builds.

**How do I deal with the 4-second reload?**
Reload speed mods, In Sync gloves, or weapon-swap rotations to a fast secondary during reload windows. Most experienced Demolisher users carry a Custom 1911 or Sleipnir as the swap-target.
