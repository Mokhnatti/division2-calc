## At a Glance

The SOCOM M1A CQB is the compact variant of the M1A platform, redesigned for closer-range engagements. Where the standard M1A is a precision tool for medium-to-long range, the SOCOM trades a sliver of per-shot damage and effective range for a higher rate of fire and a larger magazine. With 90,000 base damage, 140 RPM, and a 10-round magazine, the SOCOM M1A CQB is the best rifle for hybrid playstyles that mix close-quarters aggression with mid-range precision.

In TU22.1 the SOCOM M1A CQB has emerged as a strong alternative to the standard M1A for players who want more sustained pressure. The 10-round magazine erases the M1A's reload pressure on add waves, and the 140 RPM cadence is fast enough to compete with assault rifles at close range. Per-shot damage is only marginally lower than the M1A, so the trade-off favors most playstyles.

### Damage math

Sustained body-shot DPS equals 140 / 60 multiplied by 90,000, giving 210,000 raw DPS - slightly higher than the standard M1A. Apply the 2.0x headshot multiplier with typical 50 percent crit chance and 80 percent crit damage, and the SOCOM reaches roughly 700,000 to 800,000 DPS on headshot-only fire. With Strained or Rifleman, the SOCOM climbs above 1.4 million sustained DPS in optimal-range engagements.

## Talent slot

The SOCOM M1A CQB carries a single mod talent slot. The strongest TU22.1 picks:

- **Strained** - up to 50 percent critical hit damage in exchange for armor. The SOCOM's higher cadence means more crits per second, increasing Strained's effective uptime.
- **Rifleman** - up to 65 percent weapon damage stacking on consecutive headshots. The larger magazine helps maintain stacks.
- **Perfectly Optimist** - up to 35 percent weapon damage as the magazine empties. Niche on a 10-round magazine but viable.

Strained for damage builds, Rifleman for forgiveness and stacking sustain.

## Top Builds

### 1. Strained SOCOM (PvE Heroic)

Stack Critical Hit Chance and Critical Hit Damage on chest and backpack, Picaro's Holster, Glass Cannon chest. Strained on the SOCOM produces high crit damage numbers, and the 140 RPM cadence delivers them quickly. Output: 1.4 to 1.7 million DPS sustained.

### 2. Rifleman Sustained

Rifleman rewards consecutive headshots, and the SOCOM's 10-round magazine makes stacking easier than on the standard M1A. Use a Coyote's Mask, Memento backpack, and Determined chest. Output: 1.4 to 1.6 million DPS sustained, with strong stack sustain.

### 3. Hunter's Fury PvP Rifleman

For PvP, Hunter's Fury empowers fast pushes with armor regeneration on melee. The SOCOM's high cadence and 10-round magazine make it the best rifle for pushing through duels. Strained on the gun, Coyote's Mask on the head.

## PvE vs PvP

In PvE, the SOCOM M1A CQB is a top-tier rifle for Heroic content with significant add density. Its higher cadence and larger magazine make it more forgiving than the standard M1A on add waves, and Strained or Rifleman procs hit just as hard. On bosses with weak-point mechanics, the SOCOM is slightly behind the M1A because of the lower per-shot damage.

In PvP, the SOCOM is one of the best close-range rifles in the game. The 140 RPM cadence outpaces the M1A in duels, and the 10-round magazine allows for more follow-up shots if your first burst misses. Time-to-kill on a 6-million-armor opponent is roughly 0.5 seconds with two headshots. The SOCOM is the rifle to bring to Conflict and Dark Zone duels at close range.

## FAQ

### Is the SOCOM M1A CQB better than the standard M1A?

For sustained DPS and add clearing, yes. For per-shot damage and long-range engagements, the standard M1A wins. The SOCOM is the rifle to bring for Heroic add waves and PvP duels; the M1A is the rifle to bring for boss fights and long-range scenarios.

### Strained or Rifleman?

Strained for damage builds, Rifleman for stacking sustain. Strained produces the highest single-shot crits, Rifleman the highest sustained DPS over long fights. Most players keep both rolls.

### What attachments should I prioritize?

Critical-hit-chance optic, critical-hit-damage muzzle, extended magazine (13 rounds), and a stability grip. The SOCOM's standard 10-round magazine can be swapped for a 13-round magazine.

### How does the SOCOM compare to the Police M4?

Police M4 is a named M1A variant with Perfectly Allegro (rate of fire on weapon kill). The SOCOM has a higher base cadence than the Police M4 but lacks the named talent. The SOCOM is more flexible for talent rolls.

## Closing

The SOCOM M1A CQB is the rifle for players who want close-range aggression without giving up rifle-class per-shot damage. It is the highest-cadence legendary rifle in the game, the most forgiving in adds-heavy content, and a top pick for PvP duels at close range. Per-shot damage is slightly lower than the standard M1A, but the higher cadence and larger magazine more than make up for it in most scenarios. In TU22.1 the SOCOM sits in the S tier of legendary rifles for sustained content and PvP, with only the standard M1A ahead on long-range and one-shot scenarios. Pick the SOCOM if you want a rifle that plays at AR ranges, and pair it with Strained or Rifleman for the strongest results.
