## At a Glance

The Police Mk16 is the workhorse assault rifle in The Division 2. Firing at 700 RPM with a thirty-round magazine and a 50k base damage value, the Police Mk16 produces the cleanest balance of fire rate, magazine, and per-shot damage in the AR family. The x1.65 headshot multiplier is generous for an assault rifle and pairs cleanly with headshot-scaling builds.

In TU22.1 the Police Mk16 sits in the upper-middle tier of assault rifles. It does not produce the meta sustained DPS of an Eagle Bearer and it does not have the burst potential of a Carbine 7, but it remains one of the most reliable AR pickups for general PvE and PvP content. The 700 RPM trigger and thirty-round magazine combine into a weapon that handles every engagement scenario without specialization.

The Police Mk16 is the assault rifle for agents who want a no-compromise general-purpose weapon. It is more accurate than a Carbine 7, faster than a P416, and more forgiving than an F2000 or AUG.

The 50k base damage is mid-range for the AR family. Combined with the 700 RPM trigger, the Police Mk16 produces about 580k damage per second of sustained fire before any build buffs apply. That figure is competitive with most named ARs and beats the F2000 and AUG on raw DPS.

The thirty-round magazine produces about 2.6 seconds of sustained fire before reload. The 2.0-second reload is the fastest in the AR family and the difference matters on extended engagements.

Headshot multiplier of x1.65 is generous for an assault rifle. It does not match the x2.0 of a rifle but it rewards consistent headshot accuracy more than the F2000's x1.55 or the AUG's x1.5.

## Talent

The Police Mk16 rolls from the base assault rifle talent pool. Best-in-slot picks are:

- **Optimist** — weapon damage increases by 1% for every missing round in the magazine.
- **Pummel** — kills refresh the magazine and grant +20% weapon damage for 10 seconds.
- **Killer** — critical hit kills grant +50% critical hit damage for 30 seconds.

Optimist is the meta talent for sustained-DPS AR builds because the thirty-round magazine produces enough headroom for the damage stacks to build meaningfully before reload. Pummel is a respectable alternative for builds that prioritize magazine refresh over delayed damage scaling. Killer is the niche pick for crit-focused builds.

## Top Builds

### Striker Police Mk16

Six-piece Striker with the Police Mk16 is the textbook solo Heroic AR build. Striker stacks build per hit and the 700 RPM trigger produces stacks faster than most ARs. The thirty-round magazine means stacks survive between targets without needing Pummel to refresh them. Optimist is the meta talent here because the damage scales as you progress through the magazine.

### Heartbreaker Police Mk16

Heartbreaker scales weapon damage with hits taken while ammo is in the magazine. The Police Mk16's 700 RPM trigger and thirty-round magazine combine into a Heartbreaker package that scales cleanly without specialized gear. Pair with Picaro's Holster and Fox's Prayer for the standard talent stack.

### Memento AR Build

Memento backpack stacks weapon damage as you pick up trophies dropped by kills. The Police Mk16's high fire rate produces kills quickly and the Memento stacks build cleanly. This is the most flexible AR build because Memento works in every game mode without configuration.

## PvE-PvP

In PvE the Police Mk16 is a strong general-purpose assault rifle. It does not produce the meta DPS of an Eagle Bearer and it does not have the burst potential of a Carbine 7, but it handles every engagement scenario without specialization. Heroic patrols, named bosses, and Hunter HVTs all melt under a Striker or Heartbreaker Police Mk16.

In PvP the Police Mk16 is a respected pick for normalized Conflict and Dark Zone work. The x1.65 headshot multiplier is the highest of any standard AR and the 700 RPM trigger gives you enough fire rate to chase moving targets. Aim for the head. The Police Mk16 produces consistent two- and three-shot kills on most agents at engagement ranges.

## FAQ

**Is the Police Mk16 better than the F2000?**
For headshot scaling and reload speed, yes. For raw fire rate, the F2000 wins. The Police Mk16 is the better all-rounder; the F2000 is the better dedicated DPS pick.

**What is the best talent for the Police Mk16?**
Optimist for sustained-DPS builds, Pummel for magazine-refresh builds, Killer for crit-focused builds. Optimist is the meta pick on most six-piece builds.

**Can the Police Mk16 one-shot reds on Heroic?**
On a clean headshot with a fully buffed Striker or Heartbreaker stack, yes, but only on weak reds. Standard reds will require two or three headshots even on a top-tier build. Body shots will not one-shot anything on Heroic.

**Is the Police Mk16 viable in PvP?**
Yes, it is one of the most reliable AR picks for normalized Conflict and Dark Zone. The x1.65 headshot multiplier and the 2.0-second reload combine into a package that handles 1v1 engagements as well as any non-exotic AR.
