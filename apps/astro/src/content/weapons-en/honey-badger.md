## At a Glance

Honey Badger is a Named Assault Rifle that hit the meta hard once players figured out the locked-roll talent and AR-class versatility made it the closest thing to a god-roll AR you can get without farming the slot for weeks. With a base damage of 45,000 per bullet, a 750 RPM cadence, a 30-round magazine, and a 2.0-second reload, this rifle slots into nearly every AR build and stays competitive against exotic ARs in the same slot. The 1.55x headshot multiplier is class-standard.

- **Who this is for.** Players who want a top-tier AR primary without committing to an exotic and want a weapon that pairs cleanly with Vigilance gear-set scaling. The premium roll selection is the draw.
- **Vigilance synergy.** The talent is specifically tuned to scale with Vigilance gear-set damage stacks, which makes it the AR of choice for that build path.
- **Acquisition.** Targeted Loot in Federal Triangle on weekly rotation, plus DZ named caches. Targeted is on a 4-week cycle but easy to roll a clean version when it comes around.
- **Best fit:** Vigilance gear set, Strikers Battlegear, Coyote's Mask holster, In Sync chest, Memento backpack.

### Intrinsic Attributes

The intrinsic on Honey Badger is the reason it is named. The premium roll selection averages 14-16% on weapon damage and an additional 10-13% on a secondary like CHD, headshot damage, or damage to armor. Targeted-loot rolls consistently sit at the high end of that range, which is why the named version is preferred over a god-roll generic AR in the slot.

### Built-in Mods

Honey Badger ships with a 4x marksman optic, a 30-round magazine, and a stability-biased underbarrel. Standard slots, no locks — you can swap any of them for build-specific optimization.

## Weapon Talent: Premium AR Rolls + Vigilance Synergy

> "Premium Rolls" — Locked attribute roll provides Weapon Damage and a secondary stat (Headshot Damage / CHD / Damage to Armor). Pairs with Vigilance gear set damage stacks for compounding bonuses.

The mechanic is a soft talent — Honey Badger does not have a flashy proc-based ability, but it does roll with locked premium attributes that match Vigilance gear-set requirements perfectly. Vigilance scales weapon damage with health and gives a damage bonus when at full armor, and Honey Badger's intrinsic damage roll plus secondary CHD or HSD plus the Vigilance flat damage bonus stacks cleanly into a 50%+ damage multiplier on standard hits.

In practice, Honey Badger is the AR you bring when you want a premium baseline rather than a procced burst. There is no kill-chain ramp, no on-reload bonus, no out-of-cover restriction — it is a high-roll AR that wins on flat numbers. That makes it especially strong in long fights where ramp talents waste their first 1-2 seconds and exotic talents punish missed shots.

Practical numbers from a verified Vigilance 4pc + Memento setup at full armor and max trophy stacks:

- Body shot, base: **~45,000** per bullet
- Body shot, Vigilance bonus: **~67,500** per bullet
- Body crit, Vigilance + Memento + Coyote: **~210,000** per bullet
- Headshot crit at full stack: **~325,000** per bullet
- Sustained DPS, full mag: **~3.5M**

The premium intrinsic roll is the reason this AR sits above generic ARs in the same slot. You are getting an extra 10-15% baseline damage just by rolling it instead of a basic AR with the same brand and chest.

## Top Builds

### Honey Badger × Vigilance 4pc

The flagship synergy build. Vigilance scales weapon damage with current health and gives a flat damage bonus when at full armor, and Honey Badger's premium roll layers cleanly on top. The build wants you to stay topped up on armor (Skilled brand for armor regen, Vigilance backpack for the chest bonus) and the resulting flat damage profile is competitive with exotic ARs in pure DPS.

### Honey Badger × Strikers Battlegear

The aggressive variant. Strikers stacks weapon damage on hits, Honey Badger's premium roll gives the per-bullet ceiling, and the 750 RPM puts you firmly in the rate-of-fire window where Strikers stacks build fastest. Coyote's Mask in the holster for swap-procced CHC.

### Honey Badger × In Sync × Memento

The hybrid skill-weapon variant. In Sync flips weapon and skill damage on alternating crits, Memento adds armor and weapon damage trophies on kills, and Honey Badger's premium roll gives a strong baseline for the weapon side of the flip. Pairs with Stinger Hive for area denial.

## PvE vs PvP

In PvE, Honey Badger is a top-3 named AR for any AR-based build. The 35m optimal range works for indoor Heroic, the 750 RPM is a sweet spot between control and DPS, and the premium roll means you do not have to optimize an exotic talent to stay competitive. Where it falls off is fighting against the absolute ceiling of exotics like Eagle Bearer or Ridgeway's Pride, where the procced talents pull ahead by 10-20% in optimal conditions.

The 30-round mag and 2.0-second reload are both class-leading, which means Honey Badger has more sustained DPS than its per-bullet numbers suggest.

In PvP, Honey Badger is a strong AR pick for skirmish and DZ landmark farming. The premium roll plus Vigilance flat bonus is competitive with exotic ARs in TTK, and the lack of a procced talent means there is no ramp delay before you start dealing real damage. Most ranked PvP players who don't run an exotic AR pick Honey Badger by default.

## Comparison

Versus Eagle Bearer, Honey Badger trades the headshot-kill talent for a flat premium roll. Eagle Bearer has higher ceiling on a Tipping Scales build, Honey Badger has higher floor on any standard AR build. Versus Ridgeway's Pride, Honey Badger is more flexible (no cover restriction) but lower ceiling at maximum talent uptime.

Versus Police M4 or AK-M (the base ARs), Honey Badger wins clean on the locked premium roll. The base ARs let you mix talents like Optimist or Boomerang that Honey Badger cannot, but the flat damage advantage makes Honey Badger the default pick for most builds.

## FAQ

**Is Honey Badger still good after TU22?** Yes. The premium roll has not been touched in any recent balance pass and Vigilance remains a top-tier gear set. Targeted rotation is on a 4-week cycle.

**What is the best secondary roll?** Headshot damage if you play the AR like a precision rifle, CHD if you stack crit chance from elsewhere, damage to armor for raid yellow burn-down. Most players prefer HSD or CHD.

**Honey Badger or a god-roll Police M4?** Honey Badger for builds that do not depend on a specific brand-chest talent. Police M4 with Optimist for sustained DPS. Different tools.

**Does Honey Badger work without Vigilance?** Yes. The premium roll stands on its own and scales with any AR build. Vigilance is just the synergy that pushes it to the ceiling.

**Can I farm Honey Badger between rotations?** DZ named caches have a small chance to drop it, and weekly DZ landmark rewards include named ARs in the pool. But targeted-loot rotation is the practical farming window.

## Closing

Honey Badger is the AR for players who want a premium baseline without committing to an exotic talent. Pair it with Vigilance for the flat damage ceiling, with Strikers for the aggressive variant, or with In Sync for the skill-weapon hybrid. It is the cleanest non-exotic AR in the slot and it earns its named status.
