## At a Glance

AUG A3-CQC is a base assault rifle with 50,000 base damage, 700 RPM, a 30-round magazine, a 2.0-second reload, and a 1.55x headshot multiplier. The CQC variant of the AUG family is built for closer engagements than the standard AUG A3 — slightly tighter recoil pattern, slightly shorter optimal range, same RPM and damage profile. With an open talent slot, AUG A3-CQC slots into any AR-flagged build that needs talent flexibility, and the close-quarters tuning makes it a natural pick for aggressive builds that flirt with SMG range.

- **Who this is for.** Players who want an AR with close-range tuning and full talent flexibility — Hunter's Fury, Strikers, Coyote's Mask aggression setups.
- **Tempo.** 700 RPM is the comfortable middle of the AR class — fast enough for close-range pressure, slow enough for medium-range control.
- **Acquisition.** World drop, very common. No farming required.
- **Best fit:** Any AR-flagged build, especially close-range setups (Hunter's Fury, Strikers, In Sync close-quarters).

### Intrinsic Attributes (random roll)

AUG A3-CQC rolls weapon damage as primary and a secondary like critical hit damage, headshot damage, or close-range damage. The god-roll target depends on the talent — for a Boomerang build, prioritize CHC and CHD; for a Strained build, prioritize weapon damage and HSD. Close-range damage as secondary scales well with the gun's natural play pattern.

### Open Talent Slot

Unlike named ARs, AUG A3-CQC has an open talent slot. Common picks:

- **Boomerang.** Critical hits return ammo to the magazine. Pairs with high-CHC builds.
- **Strained.** Weapon damage scales with critical hit damage. Pairs with stat-stick gloves and CHD-heavy rolls.
- **Optimist.** Damage scales with how full the magazine is. Pairs with mag-size rolls and frequent reloads.
- **Vigilance.** Bonus damage when shield/armor is full. Pairs with the Vigilance chest piece.
- **Killer.** Critical hits grant a stacking bonus on subsequent kills. Pairs with kill-frequent swarm-clear builds.

### Built-in Mods

Standard AR mod slots — optic, magazine, underbarrel, muzzle. The natural fit is a 4x optic, an extended magazine, a CHD or weapon-damage muzzle, and an angled grip for horizontal recoil. The CQC tuning means the recoil pattern is tighter than the standard AUG, which makes vertical grips less necessary.

## Weapon Talent: Open Slot

Because AUG A3-CQC has no locked talent, every loadout treats it differently. Five common builds and their roles:

**Boomerang** is the meta pick for high-CHC builds. At 60%+ CHC, Boomerang refills the magazine fast enough to extend mag duration past the named alternatives, and at high crit damage rolls the per-bullet output rivals named ARs.

**Strained** scales weapon damage with CHD at the cost of armor. Pairs with stat-stick gloves and zero-armor builds. Ceiling is high but survivability is low; reserved for Glass Cannon Heroic loadouts.

**Optimist** scales damage with magazine fullness — the fuller the mag, the higher the damage. Pairs with mag-size mods. Works on AUG A3-CQC because the close-range play pattern naturally has frequent reloads.

**Vigilance** provides a flat damage bonus while shield or armor is full. Pairs with the Vigilance chest piece for set-and-forget +25% damage. The most consistent "free damage" talent in the AR pool.

**Killer** grants a damage stack on critical kills. Pairs with kill-frequent swarm-clear builds. The 700 RPM at close range produces enough crits to maintain Killer stacks through any extended engagement.

Verified numbers vary by talent; example with Strikers + Boomerang at 60% CHC:

- Body shot, no crit: **~70,000** per bullet
- Body crit: **~210,000** per bullet
- Headshot crit: **~410,000** per bullet
- Sustained DPS across mag cycles: **~2.7M**

## PvE vs PvP

In PvE, AUG A3-CQC is the AR pick when the build wants close-range AR play with full talent flexibility. Hunter's Fury, In Sync, and Strikers all benefit from the AUG's tighter recoil at sub-25m engagement. The 30m optimal range is shorter than most ARs, but for builds that commit to close-range, that is a feature not a flaw — it means the gun is tuned for exactly where the build wants to fight.

In PvP, AUG A3-CQC is solid for close-range objectives. The 700 RPM is on the slow side compared to Wolverine, but the tighter recoil at close range gives it an edge in aim discipline. With Boomerang and a high-CHC roll it can extend mag duration in PvP fights, which is a small edge in long firefights.

## FAQ

**Should I use AUG A3-CQC or AUG A3 standard?**
The CQC variant has tighter close-range recoil and shorter optimal range. The standard AUG A3 has longer optimal and slightly looser recoil. For close-range builds (sub-25m), AUG A3-CQC. For medium-range (25-40m), standard AUG A3.

**What is the best talent for AUG A3-CQC?**
Boomerang for high-CHC builds, Killer for kill-frequent swarm clear, Vigilance for set-and-forget, Strained for stat-stick burst. Pick based on gear set and play style.

**What optic should I run?**
Red-dot or 4x for close-range engagements. The 30m optimal does not reward high-magnification optics.

**Is AUG A3-CQC worth using over Custom P416?**
Both are world-drop ARs with open talent slots. Custom P416 has slightly longer optimal range (35m); AUG A3-CQC has tighter close-range recoil. For Hunter's Fury and close-range setups, AUG A3-CQC. For generalist medium-range, Custom P416. Most agents keep both rolled with different talents.

**Has the AUG family been changed in TU22.1?**
No direct changes. The Striker rework and Hunter's Fury reload buff indirectly buff all ARs, and Boomerang's interaction with Striker stacks is unchanged.

## Closing

AUG A3-CQC is the close-range AR for talent-flexible builds. 700 RPM, 50,000 base damage, a 2.0-second reload, and tighter recoil at sub-25m range give it the handling profile that aggressive builds want. Slot Boomerang for high-CHC sustain, Killer for kill-frequent swarms, Vigilance for set-and-forget bonus damage — the gun adapts to the build. Less long-range than Custom P416, less concentrated than a named drop, but for close-range AR play with full talent flexibility, AUG A3-CQC is the platform.
