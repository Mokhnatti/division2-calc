## At a Glance

The AA-12 is the highest-RPM full-auto shotgun in The Division 2 and one of the most consistent boss-DPS picks in the entire shotgun family. Firing at 300 RPM with an eight-round magazine and a 30k per-shell damage value, the AA-12 produces the highest sustained shotgun DPS in the game, surpassing the ACS-12 by roughly 20% and outpacing the Super 90 by a comfortable margin.

In TU22.1 the AA-12 is firmly meta. Every shotgun-focused build that prioritizes boss damage runs the AA-12 as either the primary or the swap weapon. The 300 RPM trigger feels closer to an SMG than a shotgun and the magazine drains in well under two seconds, which makes positioning and reload management more important than on any other shotgun in the game.

The AA-12 is the shotgun for agents who can position. It punishes mistakes more than the ACS-12 because of the faster reload cycle and the harder-to-control recoil, but when it works it is the highest-damage shotgun in the game.

The 30k base damage figure is identical to the ACS-12 but the 300 RPM trigger speed produces 20% more sustained DPS. That difference is what separates the AA-12 from the rest of the family. On a Striker or Heartbreaker build, the AA-12 will out-damage every other shotgun on a yellow boss provided you can stay inside ten meters.

The eight-round magazine drains in about 1.6 seconds of continuous fire, which is the shortest sustained-fire window in the shotgun family. That short window is the reason Pummel is non-negotiable on this weapon.

The 2.0-second reload is acceptable but feels longer because of the short magazine duration. Headshot multiplier of x1.5 is standard and is harder to leverage on the AA-12 than on any other shotgun because of the trigger speed and recoil.

## Talent

The AA-12 rolls from the full-auto shotgun talent pool. Best-in-slot picks are:

- **Pummel** — kills refresh the magazine and grant +20% weapon damage for 10 seconds.
- **Lucky Shot** — magazine capacity increased by 50%, weapon handling improved.
- **Optimist** — weapon damage increases by 1% for every missing round in the magazine.

Pummel is the only meta talent on the AA-12 and arguably the only viable talent if you are not specifically building around magazine-management mechanics. The 1.6-second sustained-fire window means you will reload every two or three kills, and Pummel turns those kills into refreshes that cancel the reload entirely.

Lucky Shot is the alternative pick for sustained-DPS builds that want a twelve-round AA-12, which extends the sustained-fire window to roughly 2.4 seconds. Optimist is mathematically interesting but practically awkward on a fire rate this fast.

## Top Builds

### Striker AA-12

Six-piece Striker with the AA-12 is the meta solo Heroic build in 2026. Striker stacks build per pellet and the AA-12 produces more pellets per second than any other weapon in the game. You hit max stacks within the first half of your first magazine and Pummel keeps them alive forever. This is the highest sustained-DPS shotgun build the game has ever produced.

### Heartbreaker AA-12

Heartbreaker scales weapon damage with hits taken while ammo is in the magazine. The AA-12 drains its magazine faster than any other weapon, which makes Pummel mandatory to keep the stacks alive, but when it works the Heartbreaker AA-12 produces the highest-damage shotgun build in the game outside of full Strega Glass Cannon configurations.

### Hunter's Fury AA-12

Hunter's Fury close-range brand stacks scale with the AA-12's 300 RPM trigger more cleanly than with any other shotgun. This is the meta shotgun build for occupied Dark Zone and Conflict, where the AA-12's room-clear potential combines with Hunter's Fury crowd-control bonuses to produce the strongest close-range PvP package in the game.

## PvE-PvP

In PvE the AA-12 is the meta boss-DPS shotgun. It does not produce the per-shot one-shots of an M870 and it does not have the controllability of an ACS-12, but its sustained DPS is the highest in the family by a wide margin. Heroic Hunter HVTs, named bosses, and yellow elites all melt under a Striker or Heartbreaker AA-12 inside ten meters.

In PvP the AA-12 is a top-three pick alongside the Boomstick and the M870. The 300 RPM trigger overwhelms agents who try to disengage and the eight-round magazine gives you the option to chase. Aim for the upper chest. PvP normalization caps shotgun damage scaling, so the AA-12's sustained-DPS advantage matters more than its per-shell damage.

## FAQ

**Is the AA-12 better than the ACS-12?**
For sustained DPS, yes, by a comfortable margin. For controllability and forgiveness, no. The AA-12 punishes positioning mistakes harder than any other shotgun, but when it works it produces the highest shotgun damage in the game.

**What is the best talent for the AA-12?**
Pummel, no question. The 1.6-second sustained-fire window means you need the magazine refresh to keep the weapon online, and the 20% weapon damage buff stacks cleanly with every shotgun build in the game.

**Can the AA-12 one-shot reds?**
Not in a single trigger pull. The per-shell damage is too low. However, a two- or three-shell burst will kill any red on Heroic with a buffed Striker or Heartbreaker build.

**Is the AA-12 the best shotgun in the game?**
For sustained DPS, yes. For per-shot kill potential, no — that title belongs to the M870 Express or the Boomstick. The AA-12 is the meta choice for boss DPS and Striker builds.

## Closing

The AA-12 is the meta shotgun for sustained DPS in 2026. It does not produce the highlight-reel one-shots of a pump and it does not have the controllability of the ACS-12, but its 300 RPM trigger and eight-round magazine combine into the highest sustained-DPS shotgun the game has ever produced. On Striker, Heartbreaker, or Hunter's Fury, it is the top-tier pick for close-range engagements.

If you can position consistently inside ten meters and you can manage the reload cycle, the AA-12 is the shotgun you want. If you cannot, the ACS-12 is the more forgiving alternative.
