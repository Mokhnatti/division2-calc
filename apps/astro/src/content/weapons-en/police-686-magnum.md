## At a Glance

The Police 686 Magnum redefines pistol expectations. At 200,000 base damage and 200 RPM, this weapon delivers devastating per-shot output rivaling some rifles. The 6-round magazine enforces precision—you cannot afford missed shots. The 1.5x headshot multiplier produces 300,000 damage per critical hit, enabling single-magazine eliminations of elite targets in coordinated builds. The 2.5-second reload is lengthy, but irrelevant if you eliminate targets with 2–3 shots. This is the finisher's tool: soften targets with primary, switch to 686 for alpha strike, eliminate before reload needed.

## Stats & Performance

**Base Configuration:**
- **Damage:** 200,000 per shot
- **Fire Rate:** 200 RPM
- **Magazine Capacity:** 6 rounds
- **Reload Time:** 2.5 seconds
- **Headshot Multiplier:** 1.5x
- **Optimal Range:** 5–20 meters

**Damage Output Breakdown:**

At 200 RPM, the Police 686 generates 666 damage per second (note: this represents time-to-empty + reload cycle, not DPS against sustained targets). Total magazine damage is 1,200,000 health (pure bodyshots), or 1,800,000 with full headshots. Magazine depletion takes 1.8 seconds at full fire rate.

The 200,000 damage-per-shot is exceptional—this single round exceeds many rifles' total magazine damage per shot. The 1.5x headshot multiplier produces 300,000 damage per crit headshot. If you achieve 4 headshots per 6-round magazine, total magazine damage reaches 1,600,000 (avg)—exceeding rifles by pure burst potential.

## Talent Slot

The Police 686 accepts **Pistol talents**. Recommended configurations include:

- **Ranger:** +1% damage per meter beyond 10m. At 15m, +5%; at 20m, +10%. Compound with 200,000 base damage for massive range scaling.
- **Desperation:** +25% damage when magazine capacity drops below 25% (1.5 rounds remaining). Perfect for single-target phases where you're dumping all 6 rounds.
- **In Sync:** +25% damage while in cover and looking at marked enemies. Excellent for group farming where marking is consistent.
- **Ace:** +20% damage to the last shot in magazine. Every magazine's final round deals 240,000 damage, incentivizing full magazine bursts.

Desperation is the meta choice for finisher builds; Ranger for sustained engagement profiles.

## Top Builds

**Finisher Alpha Strike (PvE/PvP)**
Pair Police 686 with a primary AR (M4, Mk16) for softening. Build for Weapon Damage rolls and stack Critical Hit Chance to 50%. Engagement strategy: unload primary magazine (AR or rifle), switch to 686, deliver 4–6 crit headshots (1.2 million+ damage), target elimination. Use Technician specialization for skill power; deploy sticky bomb or EMP for target marking (In Sync synergy).

**Ranger Long-Range Finisher (Challenging/Heroic)**
Use Ranger talent. Engage at 15–20m with primary for initial damage. Switch to 686 for finishing shots; Ranger's +10% damage at 20m boosts 200,000 to 220,000 per shot. This playstyle suits open-field content where ranged engagements are possible.

**Crit-Heavy Elimination (PvE)**
Stack Critical Hit Damage (+140% minimum) and Critical Hit Chance (60%). Use Precision Assault talent for additional crit stacking. Single crit headshot delivers 300,000 × 2.4 = 720,000 damage with full crit scaling. Two shots eliminate most elite targets. Pair with Gunner specialization for armor regeneration per shot.

## PvE vs PvP Deployment

**PvE Strength:**
The Police 686 Magnum excels as a finisher weapon in all difficulty tiers. Against isolated elite targets, the 200,000 per-shot damage and 1.5x multiplier enable efficient elimination. In Legendary farming, the 686 serves as a "clean-up" weapon—after primary-fire softening, switch to 686 for quick target elimination before spawns overwhelm.

**PvE Limitations:**
Against multiple simultaneous targets, the 6-round magazine and 2.5-second reload are restrictive. You cannot suppress groups; you can only eliminate single targets efficiently. In Heroic hordes, use the 686 for high-priority targets (Heavies, Rushers), then switch back to primary for adds.

**PvP Viability:**
In Conflict, the Police 686 is a legitimate 1v1 threat. The 200,000 per-shot damage means headshots deal 300,000 damage—enough to eliminate or heavily damage most opponents with 2 shots. Positioning is critical; close-range (5–15m) favors the 686, while medium-to-long range (20m+) prefers rifles.

**PvP Challenges:**
Against aggressive opponents using high-RPM weapons, the 686's 6-round magazine is a liability. You must land 2–3 crits to match TTK; opponents can land 8–12 bodyshots. The 686 rewards precision; if you miss even one headshot, you lose the engagement.

## FAQ

**Q: Should I use Police 686 or a rifle as primary?**
Rifles as primary, 686 as secondary. The 686's limited magazine restricts primary deployment. Use it as a finisher after primary magazine depletion.

**Q: Does Desperation or Ranger work better?**
Desperation for burst finisher builds; Ranger for sustained engagement. If you eliminate targets within 6 shots (Desperation triggers), use Desperation. If you engage 15m+ (Ranger value), use Ranger.

**Q: Can Police 686 solo Legendary?**
No. The 6-round magazine and single-target focus make multi-enemy Legendary impossible. Use it for specific phases where single enemies appear.

**Q: What's the optimal crit chance?**
50–60%. At 50%, you achieve consistent crit triggers (3 crits per 6 shots). Above 60%, marginal returns apply. Prioritize Crit Damage scaling over additional Crit Chance.

**Q: Is 686 better than other pistols?**
Yes, for finisher builds. The 200,000 base damage exceeds all competitors. Other pistols suit sustained DPS roles; 686 is strictly a finisher weapon.

## Closing Thoughts

The Police 686 Magnum is a *precision finisher for players who value burst damage over sustained fire*. Its 200,000 base damage and 1.5x multiplier create exceptional alpha-strike potential when paired with crit builds. The 6-round magazine isn't a limitation—it's a feature that enforces discipline and positioning. In group Heroic farming (add-clear role) and coordinated PvP (1v1 finishes), the 686 competes with rifles despite being a pistol. For what it does—deliver devastating burst damage against single targets—the Police 686 is genuinely excellent. If you value precision headshots and quick eliminations, the 686 belongs in your secondary slot.
