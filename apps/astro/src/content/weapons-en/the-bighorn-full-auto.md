## At a Glance

The Bighorn (Full Auto) is the sustained-DPS variant of one of TU22.1's most popular exotic ARs. Where the 3-burst version chops up its damage into precise three-round packages, the Full Auto variant lets you ride the magazine continuously and stack the talent through high-volume fire. Same Apex Predator talent, same dirty kick to the shoulder, but tuned for players who want to hold the trigger and watch the brand stacks light up.

In TU22.1 it sits alongside the burst version as a viable top-tier AR pick, especially for builds that prioritize Striker, Sokolov, or Hunter's Fury brand benefits over deliberate burst control.

The Full Auto trade-off is clear: lower per-round damage, faster sustained DPS. Where the 3-burst variant rewards precision tap-fire, this version is built around staying on a target for one or two seconds at a time and letting the brand stacks do their work.

### Damage shape

A 33k base with 850 RPM produces a sustained DPS profile slightly above the average exotic AR but well below the burst variant on a per-shot basis. The strength is consistency. You don't have to time anything; you just hold the trigger and feed your brand stacks faster than a burst weapon could.

## Talent: Apex Predator

The same talent as the 3-burst Bighorn carries over here. The mechanic rewards sustained pressure on a target by amplifying follow-up damage as you continue firing. Where the burst version makes each three-round chunk a damage spike, the Full Auto version turns the entire magazine into a ramping pressure curve.

For players who don't want to manage burst timing, this version makes the same talent more accessible. The downside is that the per-round damage scaling is weaker, so you trade peak damage for sustained damage.

## PvE vs PvP

### PvE

In PvE, Full Auto Bighorn is a comfortable AR pick for sustained engagements. It excels in dense waves where targets keep flowing and you don't need to switch zones constantly. Countdown clears and Summit dense floors are ideal.

In raids it's a sustain weapon for boss phases that don't require zone-specific damage. The 30-round magazine and 850 RPM produce comfortable uptime between reloads.

### PvP

In PvP, the Full Auto variant is more forgiving than the burst version because you don't have to time individual bursts to ensure full damage. The damage profile is solid for medium-range duels and the recoil is manageable with attachment tuning.

## FAQ

**Does the talent work the same as the 3-burst variant?**
Yes, Apex Predator is the same talent. The difference is in the fire mode and per-round damage profile.

**Which variant should I use?**
The Full Auto variant if you want sustained DPS without burst timing. The 3-burst variant if you want maximum per-round damage and have the trigger discipline to make burst work.

**Does it scale with Sokolov or Striker brand sets?**
Both work cleanly. Sokolov scales weapon damage from sustained fire; Striker amplifies follow-up shots. The Full Auto variant feeds both faster than the burst version.

**Is it competitive in legendary content?**
Yes. The talent and damage profile keep it in the top tier of exotic ARs for sustained encounters. It's not the absolute peak but it's reliable.

**Does the burst variant outperform in raids?**
Per-shot damage is higher in the burst variant. Sustained DPS is higher in the Full Auto. Raids reward whichever you can execute more cleanly.

**Can I run it on a Glass Cannon build?**
Yes, and it pairs well because Glass Cannon doubles output and the Bighorn benefits from continuous output. Cover discipline becomes critical because Glass Cannon also doubles incoming damage.

## Closing

The Full Auto variant of The Bighorn is the version of this exotic for players who want to hold the trigger rather than time bursts. In TU22.1 it remains a strong AR pick for sustained engagements, and its talent rewards continuous fire just as cleanly as the burst version rewards precision. If you've been running the 3-burst Bighorn but find yourself wishing the cadence felt smoother, this is the alternative that gives you the same damage scaling in a continuous package. Slot it, tune the attachments for recoil, and let the brand stacks do their work.
