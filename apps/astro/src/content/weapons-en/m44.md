## At a Glance

The M44 is the base bolt-action marksman rifle in The Division 2, and it is the heaviest hitter of the legendary MMR roster. With 280,000 base damage and a 5-round magazine, every shot is a deliberate, weighty event. The 80 RPM ceiling reflects the bolt-action mechanism - you cycle, aim, and fire, with no opportunity for mashing the trigger. Reload is a slow 2.7 seconds, the longest in the MMR family, but the per-shot damage rewards patience.

In TU22.1 the M44 holds the title of highest single-shot legendary marksman rifle. It is the rifle you bring when you need to one-shot a yellow elite from across a Dark Hours room, or when you want to delete a player in the Dark Zone with a single headshot. The trade-off is sustained DPS: the bolt-action cadence simply cannot keep up with the SR-1 or SVD in fights that last more than a couple of shots. The M44's identity is precision, and it punishes anyone who tries to play it like a faster MMR.

### Damage math

Sustained body-shot DPS equals 80 / 60 multiplied by 280,000, giving 373,333 raw DPS - the lowest sustained DPS of any base MMR. The story changes on per-shot damage: 280,000 base times 2.5x headshot times typical critical-hit modifiers approaches 1.4 million per single bullet on a Heroic build. With Headhunter stacking on top, individual body shots can crit for over 6 million.

## Talent slot

The M44 carries a single mod talent slot. The strongest TU22.1 picks:

- **Headhunter** - 100 percent of headshot damage as a stack to the next body shot. The M44's high per-shot damage means Headhunter procs are the highest in the MMR class.
- **Lucky Shot** - up to 28 percent magazine bonus and reload speed scaling on missed shots. Useful on a 5-round magazine.
- **Boomerang** - heal on critical hit. Niche but viable for solo play.

Headhunter is the default damage roll. Lucky Shot is the safe pick when you want forgiveness on the small magazine.

## Top Builds

### 1. Headhunter M44 (PvE Heroic)

Stack Headshot Damage on chest and backpack, Critical Hit Chance and Critical Hit Damage in attachments, Picaro's Holster, and a Vile mask. Headhunter on the M44 produces the highest single-shot damage in the game outside of exotic gear. A single body shot on a tagged yellow elite can hit over 6 million.

### 2. Strategist Marker Sniper

The Strategist gear set marks targets with weapon damage and explodes them with skill damage. The M44's high per-shot damage maximizes mark intensity. Pair with a Brazos de Arcabuz holster for reload speed and a Memento backpack for stacking weapon damage.

### 3. Tip of the Spear Burst MMR

Tip of the Spear scales grenade and signature damage with weapon damage. The M44 feeds the set efficiently because every bullet contributes a large damage number. Headhunter on the gun, Vigilance on the chest, and a Coyote's Mask round out the build.

## PvE vs PvP

In PvE, the M44 is the boss-killer marksman rifle. Its high per-shot damage and 2.5x headshot multiplier delete yellow elites in one or two bullets, and Headhunter stacks turn body shots into nukes. The trade-off is the slow cadence: in adds-heavy content you will struggle to keep up with the SR-1 or SVD. Use the M44 for boss fights, raids, and any encounter where you need a single guaranteed kill.

In PvP, the M44 is the headshot king. A clean headshot on a 6-million-armor opponent deals roughly 4 to 5 million damage on a Heroic-tier build, which is enough to one-shot most agents. The 5-round magazine and 2.7-second reload mean you must make every shot count, and the bolt-action cadence punishes whiffs. Use the M44 in PvP only if you trust your aim.

## FAQ

### Is the M44 better than the SR-1?

For per-shot damage and one-shot scenarios, yes. For sustained DPS and add clearing, the SR-1 wins. The M44 is the rifle to bring for boss fights and PvP duels; the SR-1 is the rifle to bring for Heroic clears.

### Headhunter or Lucky Shot?

Headhunter for damage builds, Lucky Shot for forgiveness. Headhunter on the M44 produces the highest single-shot numbers in the game, while Lucky Shot keeps the small magazine more forgiving for missed shots.

### What attachments should I prioritize?

Critical-hit-chance optic, critical-hit-damage muzzle, headshot-damage magazine, and a heavy grip. Range is rarely a problem because the M44's optimal range overlaps with most engagement distances.

### How does the M44 compare to the Mantis exotic?

Mantis is an exotic MMR that stacks damage on consecutive shots and includes a unique scope. The M44 outdamages the Mantis on first-shot scenarios but loses on sustained sequences where Mantis stacks build up. The M44 is the burst sniper, Mantis is the sustained sniper.

## Closing

The M44 is the marksman rifle for players who want one-shot kills. It is the heaviest hitter in the legendary MMR roster, the most punishing on misses, and the most rewarding on hits. Sustained DPS lags behind the SR-1 and SVD, but per-shot damage and Headhunter procs put the M44 in a class of its own for boss fights and PvP duels. In TU22.1 the M44 sits in the S tier of MMRs for one-shot scenarios and the B tier for sustained content. Pick the M44 when you need a guaranteed kill, and pair it with Headhunter or Strategist for the strongest results.
