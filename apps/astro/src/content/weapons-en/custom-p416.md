## At a Glance

Custom P416 is the base variant of the P416 assault rifle, with 50,000 base damage, 700 RPM, a 30-round magazine, a 2.0-second reload, and a 1.55x headshot multiplier. It is the workhorse AR — predictable recoil, balanced damage, mid-tier RPM, and an unrestricted talent slot. Where the named Custom P416 G3 locks you into a fixed talent for higher base damage, the base Custom P416 keeps the talent slot open, which makes it the choice for build-flexible setups.

- **Who this is for.** Players building around a specific talent (Strained, Boomerang, Optimist, Vigilance) who need a stable AR base and full talent flexibility.
- **Tempo.** 700 RPM is the comfortable middle of the AR class — slower than Wolverine, faster than Mania, easy to control at all ranges.
- **Acquisition.** World drop from any AR-eligible source, very common. No farming required.
- **Best fit:** Any AR-flagged build, especially talent-dependent setups (Strained, Boomerang, Vigilance pairings).

### Intrinsic Attributes (random roll)

Custom P416 rolls weapon damage, headshot damage, or critical hit damage as primary, with a similar pool as secondary. The god-roll target depends on the talent — for a Boomerang build, prioritize CHC and CHD; for a Strained build, prioritize weapon damage and HSD. Because the talent slot is open, the intrinsic rolls work alongside whatever talent you choose, so flexibility is the entire point.

### Open Talent Slot

Unlike the named Custom P416 G3, the base Custom P416 has an open talent slot. Common picks:

- **Boomerang.** Critical hits return ammo to the magazine. Pairs with high-CHC builds (Hard Wired, Striker with critical-stacked rolls).
- **Strained.** Weapon damage scales with critical hit damage. Pairs with stat-stick gloves and CHD-heavy rolls.
- **Optimist.** Damage scales with how full the magazine is. Pairs with mag-size rolls and frequent reloads.
- **Vigilance.** Bonus damage when shield/armor is full. Pairs with the Vigilance chest piece.

### Built-in Mods

Standard AR mod slots — optic, magazine, underbarrel, muzzle. The natural fit is a 4x optic, extended mag, CHD or weapon-damage muzzle, and an angled grip for horizontal recoil. Custom P416's recoil pattern is one of the cleanest in the AR class, which means muzzle mods can prioritize damage over stability.

## Weapon Talent: Open Slot

Because Custom P416 has no locked talent, every loadout treats it differently. The four most common talents and their roles:

**Boomerang** is the meta pick for high-CHC builds. At 60%+ CHC, Boomerang refills the magazine fast enough to extend mag duration past the named alternatives, and at high crit damage rolls the per-bullet output rivals named ARs.

**Strained** scales weapon damage with critical hit damage at the cost of armor. Pairs with stat-stick gloves and zero-armor builds. Ceiling is high but survivability is low; reserved for Glass Cannon Heroic loadouts.

**Optimist** scales damage with magazine fullness — the fuller the mag, the higher the damage. Pairs with mag-size mods. The mechanic rewards frequent reloads and short bursts, the opposite of how the gun naturally plays.

**Vigilance** provides a flat damage bonus while shield or armor is full. Pairs with the Vigilance chest piece for a set-and-forget +25% damage. The most consistent "free damage" talent in the AR pool.

Verified numbers vary by talent; example with Strikers + Boomerang at 60% CHC:

- Body shot, no crit: **~70,000** per bullet
- Body crit: **~210,000** per bullet
- Headshot crit: **~410,000** per bullet
- Sustained DPS across mag cycles: **~2.7M**

## PvE vs PvP

In PvE, Custom P416 is the AR pick when the build's identity comes from the talent rather than the weapon. Heroic clears with Boomerang, Strained, or Vigilance all benefit from the base P416 over a named alternative because the talent dictates the damage profile. The 700 RPM and 2.0-second reload give it forgiving handling at all engagement ranges.

In PvP, Custom P416 is competitive but not dominant. The 700 RPM is on the slow side compared to Wolverine and high-RPM SMGs. With Boomerang and a high-CHC roll it can extend mag duration in PvP fights, which is a small edge in long firefights. For pure PvP, most players run a faster AR or an SMG.

## FAQ

**Should I use Custom P416 or Custom P416 G3?**
Use the G3 if you want the locked talent (faster reload-cycle bonus damage). Use the base P416 if your build's identity is in a different talent — Boomerang, Strained, Vigilance. The base is more flexible; the G3 is more concentrated.

**What is the best talent for Custom P416?**
Boomerang for high-CHC builds, Strained for stat-stick CHD builds, Vigilance for "always-on" damage. Optimist is viable but rewards a less natural play pattern.

**What optic should I run?**
4x for general engagements, 6x for longer sightlines. The recoil at 700 RPM is gentle enough that 8x is workable for headshot-focused setups.

**Is Custom P416 worth using over named ARs?**
For any build where the talent matters more than the base damage, yes. Boomerang, Strained, and Vigilance on a base P416 outperform a named drop with a fixed talent that does not match the build.

**Has the P416 family been changed in TU22.1?**
No direct changes. The Striker rework indirectly buffs all ARs, and Boomerang's interaction with Striker stacks is unchanged.

## Closing

Custom P416 is the AR you build around a talent rather than around the weapon itself. 700 RPM, 50,000 base damage, a 2.0-second reload, and 30-round magazine give it stable handling and predictable performance across talents. Slot Boomerang for high-CHC sustain, Strained for stat-stick burst, Vigilance for set-and-forget bonus damage — the gun adapts to the build. Less flashy than its named G3 sibling, but more versatile, and the world-drop accessibility means every Division agent has had one in their stash since week one.
